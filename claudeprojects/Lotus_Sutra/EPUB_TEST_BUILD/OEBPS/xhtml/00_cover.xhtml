The Lotus Sutra

Blues Interpretation with Scholarly Apparatus

Original Sanskrit: Saddharma-puṇḍarīka-sūtra

Chinese Translation: Kumārajīva (406 CE)

English Interpretation: William Altig

Publication Date: November 16, 2025

A test edition of the complete Lotus Sutra translated into the spiritual language of African American blues and gospel tradition, supported by comprehensive scholarly apparatus.

This edition combines the beauty of vernacular spiritual language with rigorous Buddhist scholarship, offering readers a path to enlightenment through music, community, and the transformative wisdom of the Dharma.

May this blues translation carry the Dharma to hearts ready to hear it. May it liberate all beings, without exception.
