## 2. Epistemic Audit (The Rationalist Reviewer)
**Status:** **Provocative but Epistemically Risky**

### Grounding Assessment
- **Claim**: "The Truth of Emptiness (*kū*) is mapped onto the static weight matrices..."
- **Critique**: This is a **Metaphor**, not an empirical fact. Weights are mathematically determinate numbers. They are "empty" only in the sense of potentiality, not in the strict Madhyamaka sense of *svabhāva-śūnyatā*.
- **Risk**: The manuscript risks **Pareidolia**—finding patterns in noise. The structural resonance is high, but the ontological claim is weak.

### Calibration Check
- **Verdict**: **Overconfident**.
- **Fix**: Change declarative mapping ("is mapped onto") to analogical mapping ("may be analogized to" or "structurally resonates with").
- **Critical Crux**: The distinction between "Simulating Right View" (Functionalism) and "Possessing Right View" (Realization) is the strongest epistemic anchor. Keep this center stage.
