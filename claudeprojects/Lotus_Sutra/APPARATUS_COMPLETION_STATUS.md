## APPARATUS STRUCTURE

Each chapter now includes:

### 1. PHILOSOPHICAL IMPLICATIONS
Five (5) comprehensive paragraphs covering:
- **Paragraph 1**: Primary philosophical tradition (phenomenology, metaphysics, etc.)
- **Paragraph 2**: Secondary tradition (systems theory, cognitive science, etc.)
- **Paragraph 3**: Tertiary tradition (aesthetics, linguistics, etc.)
- **Paragraph 4**: Contemporary theory relevant to chapter themes
- **Paragraph 5**: Scientific/empirical grounding

**Length**: ~200-250 words per paragraph (1,000-1,250 words total per chapter)

### 2. APPARATUS SUMMARY
Structured reference containing:
- **Chapter Number**: [N]
- **Chinese Title**: [Traditional/pinyin]
- **Primary Theme**: One sentence encapsulation of core teaching
- **Key Figures**: Named disciples/entities with brief significance statements
- **Central Teaching**: Definition of major concept with Sanskrit terms
- **Structural Function**: How chapter fits within overall Lotus Sutra arc

**Length**: ~800-1,200 words per chapter
