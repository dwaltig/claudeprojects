I am writing to introduce the BLUES VERSION OF THE LOTUS SUTRA, a groundbreaking

WHAT THIS BOOK IS

The Lotus Sutra (Saddharma-puṇḍarīka-sūtra) is one of Mahayana Buddhism's most
influential texts—known for its radical message that enlightenment is available
to ALL beings, regardless of gender, status, or capacity. This translation brings
that message into the vernacular of blues and gospel, the spiritual language of
struggle, redemption, and transcendence.

Key features:

1. UNIQUE TRANSLATION APPROACH
   Only blues/gospel vernacular translation of the Lotus Sutra in existence.
   Uses African American spiritual language to convey Buddhist Dharma.
   Accessible to general readers without sacrificing doctrinal accuracy.

2. GENDER-INCLUSIVE INNOVATION
   Revolutionary text made even more contemporary.
   Amplifies the original's radical stance on women's Buddha-nature.
   Includes women bodhisattvas and paradigm-shifting moments (dragon
   princess in Chapter 12).
   Strategic language that centers women's power as wisdom agents.

3. PERFORMANCE-READY
   Designed for oral delivery (honors traditional sutra transmission).
   Blues rhythm and structure built into verses.
   Includes performance guide with pacing, emphasis, and tone notes.
   Performance passages identified (55+ minutes of strong material).

4. COMPREHENSIVELY SUPPORTED
   Includes 40-term glossary of Buddhist and blues terminology.

   Multiple reading formats (daily, weekly, deep study).
   Character guide for 14 major Buddhist figures.

   Send query on a Tuesday-Thursday morning (best timing)

   Most editors review queries Tuesdays-Thursdays

   If no response after 6 weeks, send polite follow-up
   "Dear [Name], I wanted to follow up on my query about [title]..."
   Patience is important—publishers receive many queries

   All five publishers allow simultaneous submissions
   Include in your cover email: "This is a simultaneous submission"
   If one publisher shows interest, inform the others immediately
