AUDIO PRODUCTION SPECIFICATIONS
Blues Version of the Lotus Sutra: A Gender-Inclusive Translation
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

OVERVIEW

This document provides comprehensive specifications for producing a professional
audio version of the Blues Version of the Lotus Sutra. The text is uniquely
suited for audio production due to:

✓ Blues rhythm and vernacular flow
✓ Designed for oral delivery and performance
✓ Spiritual teachings that sing when spoken
✓ Traditional sutra transmission (oral teaching method)
✓ Gender-inclusive spirituality (powerful in voice)

IDEAL NARRATOR PROFILE

Voice Quality:
- Deep, resonant, warm tone
- Ability to shift between teaching and storytelling
- Range for verse sections (musical, emotional)
- Natural blues/gospel inflection preferred
- Professional narration/audiobook experience essential

Experience:
- Professional audiobook/narration background
- Understanding of spiritual texts (preferred)
- Blues or gospel tradition knowledge (preferred)
- Ability to handle Sanskrit pronunciation
- Long-form narration experience (8+ hours)

RECOMMENDED APPROACH: Single Narrator

Single narrator creates:
- Consistency throughout all 28 chapters
- Listener relationship with narrator's voice
- Traditional sutra transmission feel (one teacher)
- Manageable production logistics

CHAPTER-BY-CHAPTER PERFORMANCE NOTES

PROSE SECTIONS (Teaching Voice):
- Read TO listeners, not AT them
- Teacher speaking to assembly
- Authority with warmth
- Clear, intentional pacing
- Breath work at natural sentence breaks
- Speed: 130-150 words per minute

VERSE SECTIONS (Blues Poetry):
- Musical, rhythmic delivery
- Blues cadence and inflection
- Emotional expression
- Call-and-response feeling (even solo)
- Speed: 120-140 words per minute
- Allow verses to SING

PARABLE SECTIONS (Storytelling):
- Engage listeners emotionally
- Paint the scene
- Narrative momentum
- Character variations subtle
- Speed: 140-160 words per minute

KEY CHAPTERS FOR AUDIO

CHAPTER 3: THE BURNING HOUSE PARABLE *** STRONG PERFORMANCE MATERIAL
- Most important chapter for audio
- Universal teaching through story
- Can be standalone 20-minute performance piece
- Urgency and emotional engagement essential
- Verses are powerful and musical

CHAPTER 12: DRAGON PRINCESS ENLIGHTENMENT *** PARADIGM-SHIFTING MOMENT
- Revolutionary moment (woman achieves Buddhahood instantly)
- Let awe and wonder be heard in voice
- This breaks every assumption
- Excellent 15-20 minute standalone piece
- Powerful gender-inclusive statement

CHAPTERS 13-15: AVALOKITEŚVARA'S COMPASSION
- Bodhisattva teachings
- Musical and rhythmic
- Multiple forms and manifestations
- Verses are strong and singable

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

Recording Format:
- Sample Rate: 48 kHz (broadcast standard)
- Bit Depth: 24-bit (reduces to 16-bit for distribution)
- Format: WAV or AIFF (lossless)
- Mono narration (appropriate for single voice)

Post-Production:
- Light compression (smooth performance)
- Warm, natural EQ
- Normalized to consistent levels (-3dB to -1dB)
- Quality control across all chapters

Audio Structure:
- Each chapter = individual audio file
- Chapter duration: 15-30 minutes
- Total production: 8-10 hours
- Clear chapter separation for navigation

DISTRIBUTION OPTIONS

Audiobook Platforms (Primary Revenue):
- Audible/ACX (50% royalties, largest market)
- Apple Books (70% royalties)
- Google Play (70% royalties)

Podcast Distribution (Building Audience):
- Weekly chapter releases (28 weeks)
- Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts
- Drive traffic to book sales

Performance Recording (Optional):
- Live performance vs. studio narration
- Different audience and market
- Authentic performance quality
- Audience energy enhances recording

PRODUCTION TIMELINE

Studio Narration (Professional):
- Recording: 1-2 weeks
- Post-production: 2-3 weeks  
- Quality control: 1-2 weeks
- Distribution setup: 1 week
- Total: 4-5 months to first sales

Live Performance Recording:
- Performance: 1 day (8-10 hours)

- Post-production: 1-2 weeks
- Quality control: 1 week
- Total: 4-6 weeks to completed
