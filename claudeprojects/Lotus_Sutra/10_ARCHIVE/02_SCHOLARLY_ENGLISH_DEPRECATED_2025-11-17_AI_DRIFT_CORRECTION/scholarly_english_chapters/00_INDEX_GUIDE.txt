COMPLETE CHAPTER LISTING (28 Chapters)
================================================================================

INTRODUCTORY CHAPTERS (1-3)
─────────────────────────────
01. INTRODUCTION
    └─ The scene at Mount Gṛdhrakūṭa, the great assembly, the Buddha's
       supernatural manifestations, and Maitreya's inquiry of Mañjuśrī

02. SKILLFUL MEANS
    └─ Mañjuśrī recounts the Buddha's past manifestations and the Buddha
       expounds the teaching that all sentient beings can attain Buddhahood

03. PARABLES
    └─ The famous "Burning House Parable" - the Buddha teaches using skillful
       means through parables rather than direct doctrine

CORE TEACHING CHAPTERS (4-7)
─────────────────────────────
04. FAITH AND UNDERSTANDING
    └─ The five hundred disciples recount their understanding and receive
       predictions of future Buddhahood

05. THE PARABLE OF MEDICINAL HERBS
    └─ The Buddha teaches that he adapts his teaching to different capacities,
       like rain on different plants

06. BESTOWAL OF PROPHECY
    └─ Predictions of Buddhahood granted to various disciples and assembly
       members

07. THE PARABLE OF THE PHANTOM CITY
    └─ The Buddha teaches through the metaphor of a conjured city guiding
       exhausted travelers

EXPANSION & EMPOWERMENT (8-9)
──────────────────────────────
08. PREDICTION FOR FIVE HUNDRED DISCIPLES
    └─ The Buddha grants predictions to five hundred disciples

09. PREDICTION FOR THOSE LEARNING AND BEYOND LEARNING
    └─ Prophecies for those in various stages of Buddhist practice

DHARMA TEACHER CHAPTERS (10)
────────────────────────────
10. THE DHARMA TEACHER
    └─ The Buddha teaches about those who transmit the Dharma teachings

THE JEWELED STŪPA & DEVADATTA (11-12)
──────────────────────────────────────
11. THE APPEARANCE OF THE JEWELED STŪPA
    └─ A magnificent stūpa (reliquary) appears in the sky containing the
       relics of a Buddha from the past

12. DEVADATTA
    └─ The story of Devadatta, the Buddha's cousin, his antagonism, and his
       ultimate prediction of Buddhahood (radical teaching of universal Buddhahood)

PRACTICE & EXHORTATION (13-19)
───────────────────────────────
13. EXHORTATION TO UPHOLD
    └─ Encouragement to uphold, recite, and practice the Lotus Sutra

14. PEACEFUL PRACTICES
    └─ The Buddha teaches practices for Bodhisattvas to cultivate

15. EMERGING FROM THE EARTH
    └─ Countless Bodhisattvas emerge from the earth to receive the Buddha's
       teaching about his true purpose

16. THE LIFESPAN OF THE TATHĀGATA
    └─ The Buddha reveals that his actual lifespan is inconceivably long and
       that he has always been present

17. DISCERNING MERIT
    └─ The Buddha teaches about the merit of hearing and believing the Lotus Sutra

18. THE MERIT OF JOYFUL ACCEPTANCE
    └─ Teaching on the merit of accepting and upholding the sutra

19. THE MERITS OF THE DHARMA TEACHER
    └─ Elaborate teaching on the merits gained by teaching and explaining the Lotus Sutra

BODHISATTVA EXEMPLARS (20-25)
──────────────────────────────
20. THE BODHISATTVA NEVER DISPARAGING
    └─ Story of a Bodhisattva who venerated all people, never disparaging them,
       embodying the teaching that all have Buddha-nature

21. THE TRANSCENDENT POWERS OF THE TATHĀGATA
    └─ The Buddha uses supernatural powers to teach and inspire

22. ENTRUSTMENT
    └─ The Buddha entreats the assembly to uphold the Lotus Sutra in the
       future after his parinirvāṇa

23. THE FORMER LIVES OF THE BODHISATTVA MEDICINE KING
    └─ The story of how the Bodhisattva Medicine King practiced the Dharma
       in previous lives

24. THE BODHISATTVA WONDERFUL SOUND
    └─ The Bodhisattva Wonderful Sound manifests in myriad forms to teach
       the Dharma to different beings

25. THE UNIVERSAL GATEWAY OF THE BODHISATTVA AVALOKITEŚVARA
    └─ The great Bodhisattva Avalokiteśvara (Goddess of Compassion) and her
       methods of delivering sentient beings through 33 different manifestations

PROTECTIVE DHARMA & CONCLUSION (26-28)
───────────────────────────────────────
26. DHĀRANĪS
    └─ Protective spells (dhāraṇīs) for those who uphold the Lotus Sutra

27. THE FORMER LIVES OF KING WONDERFUL ADORNMENT
    └─ Story of a king's devotion to his bodhisattva-teacher in a past life

28. THE ENCOURAGEMENT OF THE BODHISATTVA SAMANTABHADRA
    └─ The Bodhisattva Samantabhadra (Universal Worthy) encourages practitioners
       to uphold the Lotus Sutra through eternal commitment - final chapter
