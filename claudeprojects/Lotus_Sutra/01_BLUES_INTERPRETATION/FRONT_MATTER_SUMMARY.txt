SECTION 2: SOURCE MATERIAL

Source Material
(Bold)

This interpretation is based on Kumārajīva's Classical Chinese translation of
the Lotus Sutra (Miào Fǎ Liánhuá Jing, 妙法蓮華經), completed in 406 CE. The
original Sanskrit text is known as the Saddharma-Puṇḍarīka Sutra, a
foundational Mahāyāna Buddhist scripture.

SECTION 3: ABOUT THIS INTERPRETATION

About This Interpretation
(Bold)

This is not a literal translation but rather a creative interpretation of
Buddhist dharma presented through blues and gospel vernacular. The work
honors the classical Buddhist teachings while making the spiritual wisdom
accessible through contemporary African American musical and linguistic
traditions. The complete text includes all twenty-eight chapters, with
interpretation notes providing scholarly context and commentary on stylistic
choices.
