# Lotus Sutra: Scholarly Translation for Academic Philosophy & Metaphysics
## A 2025 Translation Project

**Project Vision**: Create the definitive English translation of the Lotus Sutra for academic philosophers, metaphysical scholars, and educated readers interested in understanding the nature of reality.

**Key Features**:
- Scholarly American-English translation from Classical Chinese
- Connections to modern physics, systems theory, and contemporary philosophy
- Comprehensive scholarly apparatus (notes, glossary, cross-references)
- All 28 chapters with complete apparatus
- Suitable for academic citation and serious scholarship

**Target Audience**:
- Academic philosophers and religious scholars
- Metaphysicians investigating reality's fundamental nature
- Scientists interested in contemplative traditions
- Educated general readers seeking rigorous philosophical engagement
