## RECOMMENDED APPROACH

### Option 1: DIY (4-6 weeks, minimal cost)
- Create front/back matter documents yourself
- Use free tools (Pandoc, Sigil, Calibre)
- Validate with EPUBCheck
- Self-publish through Draft2Digital/Smashwords
- **Best for**: Technical users, tight budget, full control desired

### Option 2: Professional Service (2-3 weeks, $300-800)
- Prepare front/back matter documents
- Send to EPUB conversion service
- Review and approve final EPUB
- Distribute through chosen channels
- **Best for**: Non-technical users, professional quality priority, faster timeline

### Option 3: Hybrid (Recommended) (5-6 weeks, $300-500)
- Create front/back matter in-house (4-5 weeks)
- Use automated converter (Pandoc/Vellum) (2-3 days)
- Professional copyediting/validation ($300-500) (2-3 days)
- DIY testing and distribution
- **Best for**: Balance of cost, quality, and control
