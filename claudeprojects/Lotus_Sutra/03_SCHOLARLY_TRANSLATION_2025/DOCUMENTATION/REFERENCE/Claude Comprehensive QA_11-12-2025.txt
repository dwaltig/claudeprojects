     ## Translation
     ### Prose Section: The Universal Merit of Single-Verse Rejoicing
     At that time, the great assembly gathered around the Buddha heard his revelation of the Tathāgata's eternal lifespan—that immeasurable,
     incalculable eons have passed since he first attained enlightenment. Countless bodhisattvas, beginning with Maitreya, experienced tremendous
     benefit from this teaching. Incalculable aeons of beings obtained the spiritual threshold of anuttara-samyak-sambodhi; thousands of times that

     thousand-worlds obtained hundreds of millions upon millions of wheel-turning mantric formulas; three thousand world-realms' worth of great
     bodhisattvas turned the irreversible dharma-wheel; twice that number developed the dharma-wheel of purity; the bodhisattva-assembly of a

       1243 +  ### Avidyā (Ignorance)
       1244 +  
       1245 +  **Sanskrit**: अविद्या (Avidyā) or 無明 (Wúmíng)
       1246 +  
       1247 +  **Definition**: Fundamental misunderstanding of reality's true nature. The root cause of suffering according to Buddhist 
            + analysis. Consists of not understanding non-self, impermanence, and interdependence. The Lotus Sutra teaches that even ignorance 
            + is eventually transcended through dharma-revelation.
       1248 +  
       1249 +  **Etymology**:
       1250 +  - *a* = not, absence of
       1251 +  - *vidyā* = knowledge, understanding
       1252 +  - Literally: "non-knowledge" or "mis-understanding"
       1253 +  
       1254 +  **Philosophical Connections**:
       1255 +  - **Plato's Allegory of the Cave**: Ignorance as being chained in darkness, seeing only shadows.
       1256 +  - **Kant's Critique of Pure Reason**: The limits of human knowledge—we can never know things-in-themselves.
       1257 +  - **Heidegger's Concealment**: Being perpetually hides itself; ignorance is primordial.
       1258 +  
       1259 +  **Scientific Parallels**:
       1260 +  - **Information Theory**: Ignorance as entropy—maximum disorder and minimum information.
       1261 +  - **Cognitive Science**: Ignorance as the absence of neurological pattern-recognition.
       1262 +  - **Evolution**: Ignorance as the early stage of evolutionary development toward consciousness.

       1412 +  ### Emptiness (Śūnyatā)
       1413 +  
       1414 +  **Sanskrit**: शून्यता (Śūnyatā) or 空 (Kōng)
       1415 +  
       1416 +  **Definition**: The absence of permanent, unchanging essence in all phenomena. Not nothingness but rather the interdependence and
            +  fluidity of all things. Understanding emptiness is considered essential for enlightenment. The Lotus Sutra implicitly teaches 
            + emptiness through revealing that even highest truths are ultimately beyond conceptual fixation.
       1417 +  
       1418 +  **Etymology**:
       1419 +  - *śūnya* = empty, void
       1420 +  - *tā* = state, condition
       1421 +  - Literally: "emptiness" or "voidness"
       1422 +  
       1423 +  **Philosophical Connections**:
       1424 +  - **Heidegger's Nothing**: The nothing that is not mere negation but the ground of becoming.
       1425 +  - **Derrida's Différance**: The endless deferral of meaning, the absence of fixed signification.
       1426 +  - **Kant's Noumena**: The emptiness of our knowledge of things-in-themselves.
       1427 +  
       1428 +  **Scientific Parallels**:
       1429 +  - **Quantum Field Theory**: Particles as excitations in empty fields; space as fundamentally empty.
       1430 +  - **Buddhist Physics**: Matter as predominantly empty space (atoms themselves are mostly empty).
       1431 +  - **Information Theory**: Emptiness as the indeterminacy prior to information actualization.
       1432 +  
       1433 +  **Chapters**: 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 16, 20, 28
