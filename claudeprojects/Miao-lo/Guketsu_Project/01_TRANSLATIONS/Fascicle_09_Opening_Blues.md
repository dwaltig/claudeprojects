## The Sixth Object: The Gear (禪定境)

### The Setup

You've dealt with the noise (Afflictions). You've fixed the broken strings (Illness). You've chased off the hecklers (Māra).

Now you start getting **good tone**. You plug into the effects loop. You find the sweet spot on the amp.

This is **Dhyāna** (Zen/Meditation). It feels good. It sounds professional.

But Miao-lo warns: **"Dhyāna is honey on a razor."**

Why? Because if you get addicted to the tone, you stop playing the song. You just sit there hitting a G-chord for three hours because the reverb sounds cool.

**That's the trap.**

---

## The Inventory: Four Types of Gear

Miao-lo opens the warehouse. He categorizes every mental state you can hit.

### 1. The Stock Pickups (Worldly Zen)
**What they are**: The basics. The "Root Zens."
- **Four Dhyānas**: Happiness, Bliss, Peace.
- **Four Formless**: Space, Consciousness, Nothingness.
- **The Vibe**: Clean, standard, reliable.
- **The Trap**: You think this is enlightenment. It's not. It's just a clean signal. It fades.

### 2. The Custom Mods (Also-Worldly-Also-Transcendental)
**What they are**: Upgraded wiring.
- **Six Wondrous Gates**: Breath control.
- **Sixteen Special Excellences**: Fine-tuning the breath and mind.
- **Transparent Light**: High-definition clarity.
- **The Vibe**: Precision. Technical mastery.
- **The Advantage**: Can take you to the gig (liberation) or just make you sound good in the bedroom (worldly).

### 3. The Pro Rig (Transcendental Zen)
**What they are**: The heavy-duty stuff.
- **Nine Thoughts**: Looking at the corpse (impurity).
- **Back-Shedding (Vimokṣa)**: Dropping the baggage.
- **Lion Sprint**: Fast, powerful switching.
- **Transcendence**: Jumping across the fretboard instantly.
- **The Vibe**: Cutting. Sharp. Gets the job done.
- **The Goal**: Ending the noise permanently (Nirvana).

### 4. The Master Class (Great Vehicle Zen)
**What they are**: The tools used by the legends.
- **Compassion Samadhi**: Playing for the audience.
- **Buddha-Recollection**: Channeling the masters.
- **Superknowledges**: Flying, reading minds (special effects).
- **The Vibe**: Infinite. Non-dual.

---

## Why We Need to Look at This

Miao-lo says:
> "If you've already entered the stream (First Abode), you don't need to worry about this. You play whatever you want."

But for the rest of us (The Five Grades/Amateurs):
**We get stuck.**

We hit the First Dhyāna (Joy) and think we're Jesus.
We hit the Fourth Dhyāna (Equanimity) and think we're dead.

We need to treat these states as **Objects**.
Put them on the workbench.
Apply the **Ten Tools** (Ten Modes).

**Don't get high on your own supply.** Use the state to see the truth.
