## Overview

Fascicle 7 completes the **Ten Modes of Contemplation** (十乘觀法), covering Modes 5-10 — the diagnostic, treatment, and finishing tools. While Modes 1-4 establish what to do, Modes 5-10 address what to do when practice isn't working and how to complete the journey.

## The Complete Ten Modes

| # | Chinese | English | Blues | Function |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | 一念三千 | 3,000 in One Thought | The Hologram | See the whole in the part |
| 2 | 發真正菩提心 | Generate Bodhi-Mind | The True Heart | Set the intention |
| 3 | 善巧安心 | Skillful Stabilization | The Good Stop | Find the resting place |
| 4 | 破法遍 | Break Through All Dharmas | The Breaking | Dismantle all constructs |
| 5 | 通塞 | Openness/Obstruction | Open Road | Diagnose blocks |
| 6 | 道品調適 | Adjust Path Factors | Tuning the Amp | Fit the situation |
| 7 | 對治助開 | Counteractive Aids | Medicine for Buzz | Targeted treatment |
| 8 | 知次位 | Know the Stages | Reading the Map | Know where you are |
| 9 | 安忍 | Patient Endurance | Playing Through | Stay steady |
| 10 | 無法愛 | Freedom from Dharma | Dropping the Raft | Release the method |

**Modes 1-4**: What to do.
**Modes 5-7**: What to do when it's not working.
**Mode 8**: Where you are.
**Modes 9-10**: How to finish.
