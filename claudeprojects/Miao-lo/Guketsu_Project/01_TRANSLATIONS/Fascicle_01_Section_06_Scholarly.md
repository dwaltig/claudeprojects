## Source-Aligned Bilingual Block

安禪而化至五品等者。
> As for "abiding in dhyana and transforming, up to the fifth grade," etc.
此出臨終行位也。
> This sets forth the practice-rank at his life's end.
不出禪定端坐取滅故云安禪而化。
> Not leaving dhyana concentration, he sat upright and entered extinction; therefore it is called abiding in dhyana and transforming.
開皇十五年自荊下鄴。
> In Kaihuang year fifteen, he came down from Jing to Ye.
至十六年重入天台。
> In year sixteen, he re-entered Tiantai.
至十七年晉王敦請出至石城。
> In year seventeen, Prince Jin earnestly invited him to come out to Shicheng.
謂徒眾曰。
> He said to the disciples:
大王欲使吾來。
> The great prince wished me to come.
吾不負言而來。
> I came so as not to fail my word.
吾知命在此故不前進。
> I know my life ends here, so I will not proceed further.
於石像前口授遺書云。
> Before the stone image, he orally dictated a final letter, saying:
蓮華香爐犀節如意留別大王。
> Lotus censer, rhinoceros tally, and ruyi are left in farewell to the great prince.
願芳香不窮常保如意。
> May fragrance never be exhausted and may all remain ever as wished.
索三衣命掃洒。
> He requested the three robes and ordered sweeping and sprinkling.
令唱法華觀無量壽二部經題。
> He ordered the chanting of the titles of two scriptures: Lotus and Contemplation of Infinite Life.
兼讚歎竟。
> And, with praises, it was completed.
時吳州侍官等二十五人。
> At that time, twenty-five people including officials from Wuzhou
見石像倍大光明滿山。
> saw the stone image double in size, with light filling the mountain.
又索香湯漱口竟。
> He again requested fragrant water and, after rinsing his mouth,
說十如四不生。
> spoke of the Ten Suchnesses and the four non-arisings.
十法界。
> The ten Dharma-realms.
四教。
> The four teachings.
三觀。
> The three contemplations.
四悉。
> The four siddhantas.
四諦。
> The four truths.
六度十二緣。
> The six perfections and twelve links of dependent origination.
一一法門攝一切法。
> Each Dharma-gate encompasses all dharmas.
吾今最後策觀譚玄。
> I now make my final urging of contemplation and discourse on the profound.
最後善寂吾今當入。
> Into final good quiescence I now shall enter.
時智朗請云。
> At that time Zhilang requested, saying:
伏願慈悲賜釋餘疑。
> I humbly pray for compassion to explain the remaining doubts.
不審何位。
> I do not know what stage [you are at].
沒此何生。
> After this passing, where will [you] be reborn?
誰可宗仰。
> Whom can we revere and rely on?
報曰。
> He replied:
汝嬾種善根問他功德。
> You are lazy in planting good roots, yet ask about another's merit.
如盲問乳蹷者訪路。
> Like a blind man asking about milk, like a cripple asking for a road.
告實何益。
> What benefit is there in telling you plainly?
雖然吾當為汝破除疑惑。
> Even so, I shall break and remove your doubts.
吾不領眾必淨六根。
> If I had not led the assembly, I certainly would have purified the six faculties.
以損己益他但位居五品。
> By diminishing myself to benefit others, I only abide at the fifth grade.
生何處者。
> As to where I shall be reborn:
吾諸師友並從觀音皆來迎我。
> My teachers and companions, all with Guanyin, have come to welcome me.
問誰可宗仰者。
> As to whom you should revere:
汝不聞耶。
> Have you not heard?
波羅提木叉是汝大師。
> Pratimoksha is your great teacher.
四種三昧是汝明導。
> The four samadhis are your bright guide.
教汝捨重擔。
> They teach you to lay down the heavy burden.
教汝降三毒教汝治四大。
> They teach you to subdue the three poisons and to regulate the four elements.
教汝解業縛。
> They teach you to untie karmic bonds.
教汝破魔軍。
> They teach you to break the mara armies.
教汝調禪味。
> They teach you to harmonize the flavor of meditation.
教汝遠邪濟。
> They teach you to keep far from deviance and to cross over.
教汝折幔幢。
> They teach you to break banners of conceit.
教汝出無為坑。
> They teach you to emerge from the pit of inert quiescence.
教汝離大悲難。
> They teach you to depart from obstacles to great compassion.
唯此大師可作依止。
> Only this great teacher can serve as your refuge.
從捨擔下即是十境。
> From "laying down the burden" onward are exactly the ten objects.
故知若不示人境觀不任依止。
> Therefore know: if one does not show people contemplation of the objects, one cannot serve as reliance.
於是教維那曰。
> Then he instructed the chant leader, saying:
人命將終聞鐘磬聲增其正念。
> When a person's life is ending, hearing bell and chime increases right mindfulness.
唯長唯久氣盡為期。
> Keep it long and continuous, taking the end of breath as the limit.
云何身冷方復響磬。
> Why wait until the body is cold and only then sound bell and chime?
哭泣著服皆不應為。
> Weeping and donning mourning clothes should all not be done.
言已跏趺。
> After saying this, he sat in full lotus.
唱三寶名而入三昧。
> He chanted the names of the Three Jewels and entered samadhi.
即其年十一月二十四日未時端坐入滅。
> In that very year, on the twenty-fourth day of the eleventh month, at wei-hour, seated upright, he entered extinction.
