## How to Use This Glossary

- **Chinese**: Original term in Chinese characters
- **Pinyin**: Romanization for pronunciation
- **English**: Translation(s)
- **Definition**: Explanation of the term
- **Location**: Where it appears in the translations (F = Fascicle, S = Section)
