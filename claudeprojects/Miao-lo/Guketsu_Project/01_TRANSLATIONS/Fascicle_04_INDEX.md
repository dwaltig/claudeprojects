## The Six Root Afflictions (根本煩惱)

1. **貪** (Greed) — Craving, desire, attachment
2. **瞋** (Anger) — Aversion, hostility, rejection
3. **癡** (Delusion) — Ignorance, confusion
4. **慢** (Pride) — Arrogance, superiority (7 types)
5. **疑** (Doubt) — Uncertainty, lack of confidence
6. **惡見** (Wrong Views) — Mistaken understandings (5 types)

---

## The Four Types of Illness (四種病患)

1. **四大不調** — Element Imbalance (earth, water, fire, wind)
2. **業病** — Karmic Illness (past karma ripening)
3. **魔病** — Demonic Illness (Māra's interference)
4. **禪病** — Practice-Induced Illness (improper technique)

---

## The Four Types of Māra (四魔)

1. **煩惱魔** — Affliction-Māra (internal afflictions intensifying)
2. **陰魔** — Aggregate-Māra (attachment to body-mind)
3. **死魔** — Death-Māra (fear of death/impermanence)
4. **天魔** — Heavenly-Māra (external interference)

---

## The Four Dhyānas (四禪)

1. **初禪** — First Dhyāna (joy and bliss with thought)
2. **二禪** — Second Dhyāna (joy and bliss without thought)
3. **三禪** — Third Dhyāna (equanimity and subtle bliss)
4. **四禪** — Fourth Dhyāna (pure equanimity)

---

## The Four Formless Absorptions (四無色定)

1. **空無邊處** — Infinite Space
2. **識無邊處** — Infinite Consciousness
3. **無所有處** — Nothingness
4. **非想非非想處** — Neither-Perception-Nor-Non-Perception

---

## Three Stages of Integration (三階段融通)

1. **初學** — Deliberate Stage (conscious application)
2. **純熟** — Natural Stage (spontaneous arising)
3. **究竟** — Complete Stage (no distinction between practice and non-practice)
