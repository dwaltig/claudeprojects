## Concept 4: The Light in the Storm (Marketable Edition)
**Theme:** Cinematic, historically accurate spiritual biography.
**Visual:** Nichiren in authentic 13th-century Tiantai robes (grey + kesa) standing on a jagged rock at Sado Island, illuminated by a divine beam of light amidst a dark storm.
**Style:** Hyper-realistic, 8k, teal & orange contrast.
**Prompt:** "Cinematic historical portrait of a 13th-century Japanese Buddhist Monk (Nichiren) standing on a snowy jagged rock in a raging ocean storm (Sado Island). He is wearing authentic Tiantai/Tendai grey robes and a kesa (monastic stole). A massive, piercing beam of golden light descends from the storm clouds, illuminating him. Dignified, resilient, spiritual. Hyper-realistic, 8k, high contrast, teal and orange atmospheric lighting. No text. Clean artwork."
