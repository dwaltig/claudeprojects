# GEMINI.md - Nichiren Gosho Project

## 1. Project Overview

The **Nichiren Gosho Project** collects and studies the writings of Nichiren Shōnin (1222–1282), the 13th-century Japanese Buddhist monk who founded Nichiren Buddhism based on the Lotus Sutra.

**Key Components:**
- **Source Texts**: Original Japanese (古語体) from Kamakura period
- **Dual Translation**: Classical Japanese + Modern Japanese/English
- **Connection**: Direct Lotus Sutra commentary and interpretation

## 2. Directory Structure

- `01_ORIGINAL_JAPANESE/`: Original classical Japanese texts
- `02_TRANSLATIONS/`: English and modern Japanese translations  
- `03_DOCUMENTATION/`: Study notes, glossaries, context

## 3. Critical Safety Protocol

- **Preserve Classical Japanese**: Handle archaic forms with care
- **Verify Encodings**: UTF-8 with proper kanji
- **Historical Context**: 13th-century Kamakura Japan

## 4. Key Gosho

- **立正安国論** (Risshō Ankoku Ron) - 1260
- **開目抄** (Kaimoku Shō) - 1272
- **観心本尊抄** (Kanjin no Honzon Shō) - 1273

## 5. Connection to Lotus Sutra

Nichiren's writings are commentaries on the Lotus Sutra. He especially emphasized:
- Chapter 16 (Lifespan) as the "essential teaching"
- The Daimoku (題目): Namu Myōhō Renge Kyō
- Zhiyi's Tiantai interpretation of the 28-chapter structure
