### The Medicine / The Alchemy
*   **Washing the Dirt:** Purification through realization.
*   **Skillful Means:** Divine Improvisation (*Upāya*).
*   **The 108 Illnesses:** The human condition.
*   **The 10 Powers:** The specific benefits of the Sutra.
