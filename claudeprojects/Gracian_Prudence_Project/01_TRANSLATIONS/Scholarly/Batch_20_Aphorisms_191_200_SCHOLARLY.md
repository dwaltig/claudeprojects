# Oráculo Manual: Aphorisms 191-200 (Scholarly)

## 191. No pagarse de "mucha Cortesía."
Do not be paid by "Great Courtesy." For it is a species of deceit. Some use it as a "spell" to engage spirits. One must not be the "victim" of another's politeness.

## 192. Hombre de "paz," hombre de "larga vida."
A man of "peace" is a man of "long life." To live is to let live. The peaceful man not only lives, but reigns. One must not be a "rebel" against the tranquility of the mind.

## 193. Atención al que entra con la ajena para salir con la suya.
Careful with him who enters with another's to leave with his own. It is the greatest strategy of the "intent." One must be a "lynx" to discover the "hidden" target.

## 194. Tener los "dictámenes" de las cosas moderados.
Have "judgments" of things moderate. For if they are "excessive," they are "deceptions." One must not be a "slave" to the first impression.

## 195. Saber "esperar."
Know how to "wait." It is the greatest art of living. Most things reach their "perfection" through time. One must not be a "tyrant" of the occasion.

## 196. Conocer su "buen Astro."
Know your "Lucky Star." For every man has his own. Some are "born" for the day, others for the night. One must know how to utilize the "turn" of fortune.

## 197. Nunca "embarazarse" con los muchos.
Never be "embarrassed" by the many. For the wise man is a "world" to himself. One must not be a "victim" of the "vulgar" sentiment.

## 198. Saber "valerse" de los "desdoros" ajenos.
Know how to "utilize" the "slights" of others. For it is a singular art. One must not be a "martyr" to another's malevolence... but a "master" of it.

## 199. Varón de "entereza."
A man of "rectitude." It is the base of the person. One must not be a "victim" of the "unjust." Integrity is the "jewelry" of the mind.

## 200. Dexar algo que "dessear."
Leave something to "Desire." So as not to be "miserable" in happiness. The "total" fruition is the "death" of the spirit.
