# Oráculo Manual: Aphorisms 291-300 (Scholarly)

## 291. No ser "infeliz" en los desdoros.
Do not be "unfortunate" in the slights. For even a slight can be a crime of folly. One must not be the "target" of everyone's malevolence.

## 292. Saber "valerse" de los "desdoros" ajenos.
Know how to "utilize" the "slights" of others. For it is a singular art. One must not be a "martyr" to another's malevolence... but a "master" of it.

## 293. Nunca hablar con la "passión" ajena.
Never speak with the "passion" of another. For it is to be a slave to another's humor. One must not be the "echo" of another's malevolence.

## 294. Conocer su "buen Astro."
Know your "Lucky Star." For every man has his own. One must know how to utilize the "turn" of fortune.

## 295. Nunca "embarazarse" con los muchos.
Never be "embarrassed" by the many. For the wise man is a "world" to himself. One must not be a "victim" of the "vulgar" sentiment.

## 296. Dexar algo que "dessear."
Leave something to "Desire." So as not to be "miserable" in happiness. The "body" itself needs the "hope" of more.

## 297. Varón de "entereza."
A man of "rectitude." It is the base of the person. Integrity is the "jewelry" of the mind.

## 298. Nunca "ostentar" sus prendas.
Never "ostentate" your qualities. For the more they are shown, the less they are esteemed. Affectation is the "soul" of the artificial.

## 299. Saber "contradezir" con advertencia.
Know how to "contradict" with warning. It is a singular art: to show yourself "contrary" to secure the will.

## 300. En una palabra, "Santo."
In a word, be a Saint. Which is to say everything at once. Virtue is the "link" of all perfections, and the "center" of all happinesses. It makes a man prudent, attentive, wise, valiant, reported, integral, happy, plausible, true, and a "universal hero."
