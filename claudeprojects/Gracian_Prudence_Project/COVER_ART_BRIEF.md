# Cover Art Brief: The Art of Prudence

## Book Details
- **Title**: The Art of Prudence: Practical Blues Edition
- **Subtitle**: 300 Aphorisms of Strategic Wisdom
- **Author**: William Altig (Inspired by Baltasar Gracián)
- **Content**: 17th-century Spanish Jesuit wisdom rendered in gritty, modern street language

---

## Design Concept: "The Chess King's Shadow"

### Visual Elements
1. **Central Image**: A stylized chess king piece (gold or ivory)
   - Casting a dramatic long shadow
   - Shadow transforms into a crown silhouette
   - Symbolizes: strategy, power, the game of life

2. **Background**: Deep, moody atmosphere
   - Primary: Dark burgundy/wine (#4A0E0E)
   - Accent: Antique gold (#C4A052)
   - Texture: Subtle aged parchment overlay

3. **Frame/Borders**: Ornate Baroque gold filigree
   - Spanish Golden Age manuscript aesthetic
   - Not overwhelming—elegant restraint

4. **Typography Zone**: Clean space at top third for title placement
