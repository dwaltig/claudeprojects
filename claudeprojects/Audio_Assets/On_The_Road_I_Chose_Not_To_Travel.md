# On The Road I Chose Not To Travel
## The William Altig Choir

---

### Production Brief

**Genre:** Romantic Melancholy Bolero  
**Mood:** Nostalgic, contemplative, resigned  
**Tempo:** Slow, intimate  

**Instrumentation:**
- Delicate nylon-string guitar (lead, acoustic)
- Gentle congas (sparse, rhythmic foundation)
- Sparse piano flourishes (melodic accents)
- Warm strings (swell subtly in chorus)
- Subdued brass (dramatic flourishes, muted trumpet in outro)

**Arrangement Notes:**
- Opens with solo nylon-string guitar and gentle congas
- Piano enters with sparse flourishes in verse 1
- Strings swell during chorus, adding emotional tension
- Brass adds dramatic punctuation in bridge
- Outro: intimate instrumental fade with guitar, soft congas, single muted trumpet
