# Mūlamadhyamakakārikā - Chapter 21
# Saṃbhava-vibhava-parīkṣā (Examination of Arising and Passing Away)

21.1
vinā vā saha vā nāsti vibhavaḥ saṃbhavena vai |
vinā vā saha vā nāsti saṃbhavo vibhavena vai ||

21.2
bhaviṣyati kathaṃ nāma vibhavaḥ saṃbhaṃ vinā |
vinaiva janma maraṇaṃ vinaivārambhato vyayaḥ ||

21.3
saṃbhavenaiva vibhavaḥ katham saha bhaviṣyati |
na janmamaraṇaṃ caiva tulyakālaṃ hi vidyate ||

21.4
bhaviṣyati kathaṃ nāma saṃbhavo vibhavaṃ vinā |
anityatā hi bhāveṣu na kadācin na vidyate ||

21.5
vibhavenaiva saṃbhavaḥ kathaṃ saha bhaviṣyati |
na janmamaraṇaṃ caiva tulyakālaṃ hi vidyate ||

21.6
sahānyonyena vā siddhir vinānyonyena vā yayoḥ |
na vidyate tayoḥ siddhiḥ kathaṃ nu khalu vidyate ||

21.7
kṣayasya saṃbhavo nāsti nākṣayasyāsti saṃbhavaḥ |
kṣayasya vibhavo nāsti nākṣayasyāsti vibhavaḥ ||

21.8
saṃbhavo vibhavaś caiva na sato nāpy asataḥ |
saṃbhavo vibhavaś caiva na sataś cāsatash ca hi ||

21.9
saṃbhavo vibhavaś caiva na svataḥ parato na ca |
saṃbhavo vibhavaś caiva na dvābhyām ahetutaḥ ||

21.10
dṛśyate saṃbhavaś caiva vibhavaś caiva te mataḥ |
dṛśyate mohadṛṣṭīnāṃ saṃbhavo vibhavaś ca hi ||

21.11
dṛśyate saṃbhavaś caiva na vibhavena bādhyate |
na saṃbhavena vibhavaḥ kāla bhedena yujyate ||

21.12
na saṃbhavād bhavet kiṃ cin na saṃbhavati kiṃ cana |
na vibhavād bhavet kiṃ cin na vibhavati kiṃ cana ||

21.13
bhavāṃś ced āha bhāvānāṃ sāntir eva na saṃbhavaḥ |
yadi kaścid bhaved bhāvaḥ sāntir eva bhaved iha ||

21.14
bhāvam abhyupapannasya śāśvatocchedadarśanam |
prasajyate sa bhāvo hi nityo 'nityo 'pi vā bhavet ||

21.15
saṃbhavaṃ vibhavaṃ caiva yaḥ pratītya prajāyate |
saṃbhavaṃ vibhavaṃ caiva tam evāhuḥ kṣayaṃ janāḥ ||

21.16
saṃbhavo vibhavaś caiva yadi syātāṃ svabhāvataḥ |
parabhāve na dṛṣyeta saṃbhavo vibhavaś ca hi ||

21.17
parabhāve 'bhyupagate saṃbhavo vibhavaḥ kutaḥ |
ekasyārthasya nānātvaṃ kutaḥ saṃbhavavibhavau ||

21.18
saṃbhavo vibhavaś caiva na kaścid upapadyate |
saṃbhavo vibhavaś caiva kathaṃ kaścid bhaviṣyati ||

21.19
hetur na jāyate tāvat phalaṃ kva janiṣyati |
hetum vinaiva yat kāryaṃ tathā bījād ṛte 'ṅkuraḥ ||

21.20
bījaṃ na jāyate tāvad aṅkuraḥ kva janiṣyati |
bījam vinaiva yo 'ṅkuraḥ so 'hetukaḥ prasajyate ||

21.21
saṃbhavo vibhavaś caiva nityam ātmani dṛśyate |
saṃbhavo vibhavaś caiva vyākhyāto 'nena karmaṇā ||
