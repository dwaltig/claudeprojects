# Mūlamadhyamakakārikā - Chapter 20
# Sāmagrī-parīkṣā (Examination of Combination)

20.1
hetoś ca phalasya ca yadi hi saṃyogaḥ kalpate |
jayeta saṃyogaḥ kas tasmād dhetuphalayoḥ ||

20.2
hetoś ca phalasya ca yadi hi saṃyogaḥ kalpate |
jayeta saṃyogaḥ ko 'nyaḥ tasmād dhetuphalayoḥ ||

20.3
saṃyogaḥ punar ātmanā yadi syāt hetuphalayoḥ |
syātāṃ hetuphale nūnaṃ viyukte saṃgate tathā ||

20.4
viyukte yadi ca syātāṃ hetuphale katham cana |
tatra kasmāt kalpate 'yaṃ saṃyogo hetuphalayoḥ ||

20.5
yathā ca hetuphalayoḥ saṃyogo na vidyate |
tathā hetuphalayor vibhāgo 'pi na vidyate ||

20.6
vibhāge hi sati syātāṃ hetuphale katham cana |
tatra kasmāt kalpate 'yaṃ vibhāgo hetuphalayoḥ ||

20.7
phalaṃ hetusamādhānaṃ yadi syān na prabhavati |
na hetusamādhānaṃ phalaṃ yadi na prabhavati ||

20.8
na ca hetusamādhānaṃ phalaṃ yadi prabhavati |
aniruddhe 'pi hetau syāt phalam hetur niruddha-vat ||

20.9
nirodhe sati hetoś ca phalaṃ yady upapadyate |
hetur niruddhaḥ kasya syāt phalam utpadyate kadā ||

20.10
atīto hetoś ca phalaṃ yady upapadyate |
hetur niruddhaḥ kasya syāt phalam utpadyate kadā ||

20.11
yadi saṃyuktāddhetoḥ phalam utpadyate yadi |
utpadyamānaṃ tad dhetor nivarteta katham punaḥ ||

20.12
athāsaṃyuktāddhetoḥ phalam utpadyate yadi |
utpadyamānaṃ tad dhetor nivarteta katham punaḥ ||

20.13
śāśvataś cāśāśvataś ca hetuḥ phalaṃ na yujyate |
sadasadvyaktirūpeṇa kutaḥ phalaṃ bhaviṣyati ||

20.14
sataś ca phalasya hetur akriyaḥ kiṃ kariṣyati |
asataś ca phalasya hetur akriyaḥ kiṃ kariṣyati ||

20.15
na sataś cāsataś cāpi phalam utpadyate yadi |
kutaḥ sadasator hetur utpādayiṣyati ||

20.16
hetuḥ phalasya nūnaṃ syād asataḥ katham arhati |
hetuḥ phalasya nūnaṃ syāt sataḥ katham arhati ||

20.17
na cāpi nirudhyamānaḥ phalaṃ hetur janayiṣyati |
na cāpy anirudhyamānaḥ phalaṃ hetur janayiṣyati ||

20.18
nopatiṣṭhati saṃsāre phalaṃ hetur niruddha-vat |
nopatiṣṭhati saṃsāre phalaṃ hetur aniruddha-vat ||

20.19
hetuphalayoḥ saṃbandhaḥ phalasya ca na vidyate |
saṃbandhe sati hetoś ca phalasya ca na vidyate ||

20.20
ekatve kāraṇaṃ cāpi phalaṃ cāpi na vidyate |
pṛthaktve kāraṇaṃ cāpi phalaṃ cāpi na vidyate ||

20.21
phalaṃ svabhāvasadbūtaṃ kiṃ hetur janayiṣyati |
phalaṃ svabhāvāsadbūtaṃ kiṃ hetur janayiṣyati ||

20.22
na cājanayamānasya hetoḥ sadbhāva ucyate |
asadbhāvaḥ kuto hetoḥ phalaṃ janayiṣyati ||

20.23
na saṃsargāddhetuphalayoḥ phalaṃ saṃpadyate yadi |
na cāsaṃsargāddhetuphalayoḥ phalaṃ saṃpadyate yadi ||

20.24
saṃyoge cāsaṃyoge ca hetuphalayor ayuktatā |
parīkṣitā katham nūnaṃ sāmagrī janayiṣyati ||
