# Mūlamadhyamakakārikā - Chapter 23
# Viparyāsa-parīkṣā (Examination of Perversions)

23.1
saṃkalpaprabhavo rāgo dveso mohaś ca kathyate |
śubhāśubhaviparyāsān saṃbhavanti pratītya hi ||

23.2
śubhāśubhaviparyāsāḥ saṃbhavanti pratītya ye |
te svabhāvān na vidyante tasmāt kleśā na tattvataḥ ||

23.3
ātmano 'stitvanāstitve na kadācid na kva cit |
tasya tv asati kasyeme kleśāḥ karmāṇi vā bhavet ||

23.4
saṃkalpo na kṛto yena na kasyāstiti kalpitaḥ |
kasya tat kṛtrimā kleśā utpatsyante 'vasthitāḥ ||

23.5
śubhāśubhaviparyāsān kiṃcid dhi na vidyate |
viparyāsaiḥ kuto jātaḥ kleśāḥ santy ahetukāḥ ||

23.6
rūpaśabdarasasparśā gandhā dharmāś ca kevalāḥ |
gandharvanagarākārā marīcisvapnasaṃnibhāḥ ||

23.7
aśubhaṃ vā śubhaṃ vāpi kutas teṣu bhaviṣyati |
māyāpuruṣakalpeṣu pratibimbasameṣu ca ||

23.8
anāpekṣya śubhaṃ nāsty aśubhaṃ prajñapayemahi |
yat pratītya śubhaṃ tasmāc chubhaṃ naivopapadyate ||

23.9
anāpekṣyāśubhaṃ nāsti śubhaṃ prajñapayemahi |
yat pratītyāśubhaṃ tasmād aśubhaṃ naivopapadyate ||

23.10
avidyamāne ca śubhe kuto rāgo bhaviṣyati |
aśubhe 'vidyamāne ca kuto dveṣo bhaviṣyati ||

23.11
anitye nityam ity evaṃ yadi grāho viparyayaḥ |
nānityam vidyate śūnye kuto grāho viparyayaḥ ||

23.12
anitye nityam ity evaṃ yadi grāho viparyayaḥ |
anityam ity api grāhaḥ śūnye kim na viparyayaḥ ||

23.13
yena gṛhṇāti yo grāho gṛhītā yacca gṛhyate |
upaśāntāni sarvāṇi tasmād grāho na vidyate ||

23.14
avidyamāne grāhe ca mithyā vā samyag eva vā |
bhavet kasya viparyāsaḥ kasya vāviparyayaḥ ||

23.15
na cāpi viparītasya saṃbhavanti viparyayāḥ |
na cāpy aviparītasya saṃbhavanti viparyayāḥ ||

23.16
na viparyasyamānasya saṃbhavanti viparyayāḥ |
vimṛśasva svayam kasya saṃbhavanti viparyayāḥ ||

23.17
na cāsti viparītasya saṃbhavanti viparyayāḥ |
na ca kasyacit viparyāsāḥ katham na vidyate ||

23.18
na svatotpadyate bhāvaḥ parato nāpi jāyate |
na svataḥ parataś caiva viparyāsaḥ kuto nu saḥ ||

23.19
ātmā ca śuci nityaṃ ca sukham ca yadi vidyate |
ātmā ca śuci nityaṃ ca sukham ca na viparyayaḥ ||

23.20
nātmā ca śuci nityaṃ ca sukhaṃ ca yadi vidyate |
nātmā ca śuci nityaṃ ca sukhaṃ cety aviparyayaḥ ||

23.21
evam nirudhyate 'vidyā viparyāsanirodhanāt |
avidyāyāṃ niruddhāyāṃ saṃskārādyam nirudhyate ||

23.22
yadi kaścid bhaved bhāvaḥ kleśas tasya bhavet sa ca |
na kaścid asti bhāvo hi kleśas kasya bhaviṣyati ||

23.23
svakāyādṛṣṭivat kleśāḥ kliṣṭe santi na pañcadhā |
svakāyādṛṣṭivat kliṣṭaṃ kleśeṣv api na pañcadhā ||

23.24
svabhāvato na vidyante śubhāśubhaviparyayāḥ |
pratītya katham utpannāḥ kleśā rūpādayo yathā ||

23.25
rāgadveṣamohāś caivaṃ na santi paramārthataḥ |
kleśāś ca karmaṇaś caiva dehe santi na dehinām ||
