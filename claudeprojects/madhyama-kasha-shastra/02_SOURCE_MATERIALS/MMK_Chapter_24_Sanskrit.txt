# Mūlamadhyamakakārikā - Chapter 24
# Ārya-satya-parīkṣā (Examination of the Noble Truths)

24.1
yadi śūnyam idaṃ sarvam udayo nāsti na vyayaḥ |
catūrṇām āryasatyānāṃ abhāvas te prasajyate ||

24.2
parijñā ca prahāṇaṃ ca bhāvanā sākṣikarma ca |
catūrṇām āryasatyānāṃ abhāvān nopapadyate ||

24.3
tadabhāvān na vidyante phalāni vimalāni ca |
phalābhāve phalasthā no na santi pratipannakāḥ ||

24.4
saṃgho nāsti na cāstīti saṃghe cāpi na vidyate |
dharmābhāvāc ca saddharmo nāsti kuta eva nu ||

24.5
dharmaṃ saṃghaṃ ca bādhasve tathā cāryasatyāny api |
evaṃ buddhādīn api bādhasve trayaratnaphalāny api ||

24.6
śūnyatām upadhāryevaṃ sarvaṃ bādhasva tulyam |
sarvaṃ eva hi bādhasva śūnyatām upadhārayan ||

24.7
atra brūmaḥ na vijñāsi śūnyatāṃ śūnyatārthaṃ ca |
śūnyatāṃ śūnyatārthaṃ ca tata evaṃ vihanyase ||

24.8
dve satye samupāśritya buddhānāṃ dharmadeśanā |
lokasaṃvṛtisatyaṃ ca satyaṃ ca paramārthataḥ ||

24.9
ye 'nayor na vijānanti vibhāgaṃ satyayor dvayoḥ |
te tattvaṃ na vijānanti gambhīre buddhaśāsane ||

24.10
vyavahāram anāśritya paramārtho na deśyate |
paramārtham anāgamaṃ nirvāṇaṃ nādhigamyate ||

24.11
vināśayati durdṛṣṭā śūnyatā mandamedhasam |
sarpo yathā durgṛhīto vidyā vā duṣprasādhitā ||

24.12
atas ca pratyudāvṛttaṃ cittaṃ deśayituṃ muneḥ |
dharmaṃ matvāsya dharmasya mandair durvigāhatām ||

24.13
śūnyatāyām adhilayaṃ yaḥ kuruṣe tvaṃ sa te na hi |
saṃbhavaty upapattiś ca tasmāc chūnye na vidyate ||

24.14
sarvaṃ ca yujyate tasya śūnyatā yasya yujyate |
sarvaṃ na yujyate tasya śūnyata yasya na yujyate ||

24.15
sa tvam ātmany eva doṣān asatkṛtyādhirūpayasi |
aśvam evābhirūḍhaḥ sann aśvam evāsi vismṛtaḥ ||

24.16
yadi svabhāvataḥ syātāṃ bhāvānāṃ samavasthitiḥ |
ahetupratyayān bhāvān evam evaṃ prasajyate ||

24.17
kāryaṃ ca kāraṇaṃ caiva kartāraṃ karaṇaṃ kriyām |
utpādaṃ ca nirodhaṃ ca phalaṃ ca pratibādhasve ||

24.18
yaḥ pratītyasamutpādaḥ śūnyatāṃ tāṃ pracakṣmahe |
sā prajñaptir upādāya pratipat saiva madhyamā ||

24.19
apratītya samutpanno dharmaḥ kaścin na vidyate |
yasmāt tasmād aśūnyo 'pi dharmaḥ kaścin na vidyate ||

24.20
yady aśūnyam idaṃ sarvam udayo nāsti na vyayaḥ |
catūrṇām āryasatyānāṃ abhāvas te prasajyate ||
