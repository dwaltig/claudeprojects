# Mūlamadhyamakakārikā - Chapter 22
# Tathāgata-parīkṣā (Examination of the Tathāgata)

22.1
skandhā na nānyaḥ skandhebhyo nāsmin skandhā na teṣu saḥ |
tathāgataḥ skandhavān na katamo 'tra tathāgataḥ ||

22.2
buddhaḥ skandhān upādāya yadi nāsti svabhāvataḥ |
svabhāvataś ca yo nāsti kutaḥ sa parabhāvataḥ ||

22.3
pratītya parabhāvaṃ yaḥ so 'nātmā katham ucyate |
svabhāvena ca yo nāsti katham sa parabhāvataḥ ||

22.4
yadi svabhāvas tasyāsti skandhān akṛtya saṃbhavaḥ |
svabhāve 'sati nānātvam skandhebhyas tasya kim kṛtam ||

22.5
na svabhāvena tat hyeṣa nāsti cāsau svabhāvataḥ |
parabhāve svabhāve ca yasya nāsti sa vidyate ||

22.6
skandhān upādāya ca yo nāsti so 'sty anupādataḥ |
yo nāsty anupādāya ca sa bhāvo nopalabhyate ||

22.7
na bhavaty anupādāno na ca bhavati sopādāno |
na cāsti kaścit tasmāt tathāgato na vidyate ||

22.8
skandhā yadi cānupādāya na bhavati tathāgataḥ |
sa idānīm upādāya katham bhavati sopādānaḥ ||

22.9
yaś cānupādāno nāsti so 'pādānam katham punaḥ |
upādadīta kenāpi kiṃ cid gṛhṇāti kaḥ katham ||

22.10
iti hetuphalābhāvāt na grahaṇaṃ na ca gṛhyate |
tad abhāvān nirupādāno gṛhītā naiva vidyate ||

22.11
śūnyam iti na vaktavyam aśūnyam iti vā bhavet |
ubhayaṃ nobhayaṃ ceti prajñapty arthaṃ tu kathyate ||

22.12
śāśvatāśāśvatādy atra kutaḥ śānte catuṣṭayam |
antānantādi cāpy atra kutaḥ śānte catuṣṭayam ||

22.13
yena grāho gṛhītas tu ghano 'stīti tathāgataḥ |
kalpayan nirvṛte 'py asmin kalpayet so 'pi vā punaḥ ||

22.14
tathāgato yatsvabhāvas tat svabhāvam idaṃ jagat |
tathāgato niḥsvabhāvo niḥsvabhāvam idaṃ jagat ||

22.15
prapañcayanti ye buddhaṃ prapañcātītam avyayam |
te prapañcāhatāḥ sarve na paśyanti tathāgatam ||

22.16
tathāgato yatsvabhāvas tat svabhāvam idaṃ jagat |
tathāgato niḥsvabhāvo niḥsvabhāvam idaṃ jagat ||
