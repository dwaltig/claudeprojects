# Mūlamadhyamakakārikā - Chapter 17
# Karma-phala-parīkṣā (Examination of Action and Fruit)

17.1
ātmasaṃyamakaṃ cetaḥ parānugrāhakaṃ ca yat |
maitram sa dharmas tad bījam phalasya pretya ceha ca ||

17.2
cetanā cetayitvā ca karmoktaṃ paramaṛṣiṇā |
tasyānekavidho bhedaḥ karmaṇaḥ parikīrtitaḥ ||

17.3
tatra yac cetanākhyaṃ tu karma tan mānasam smṛtam |
cetayitvā ca yat tūktaṃ tat kāyikaṃ vācikaṃ ca ||

17.4
vāgvispando 'viratayo yāścāvijñaptisaṃjñitāḥ |
avijñaptaya evānyāḥ smṛtā viratayas tathā ||

17.5
paribhogānvayaṃ puṇyam apuṇyaṃ ca tathaiva ca |
cetanā ceti saptaite dharmāḥ karmāñjanāḥ smṛtāḥ ||

17.6
tiṣṭhaty ā pākakālāc cet karma tan nityatām iyāt |
niruddhaṃ cen niruddhaṃ sat kiṃ phalaṃ janayiṣyati ||

17.7
yo 'kuraḥ prādurbhavati bījād bhagnaṃ nirundhataḥ |
tatas tu phalam utpanno bījād dhi na bhavet tataḥ ||

17.8
bījāc ca yasmāt prabhavaty aṅkuro 'ṅkurāc ca phalam |
bījāt phalaṃ tasmād ucchedādisamanvitam ||

17.9
tathaiva pūrvāc cittāt saṃtānaḥ pravarttate |
saṃtānāt prabhavaty anyat phalaṃ na bhavet tataḥ ||

17.10
pūrvāc cittāc ca yasmāt pravarttate cittasantatiḥ |
tataḥ phalaṃ tasmād ucchedādisamanvitam ||

17.11
dharmāḥ śuddhaś ca kṛṣṇāś ca karmety uktaṃ daśādhikāḥ |
phalaṃ ca dharmasattvasya teṣām uktam ihaiva ca ||

17.12
yadi kṛtvā vinaṣṭaṃ tan nānuyuktaṃ bhavet phalam |
boddhavyaṃ pratipakṣeṇa tan manye doṣa isyate ||

17.13
vipraṇāśo 'vipraṇāśo yasmād eva prakalpitaḥ |
tasmād eva syād doṣa uktas tatra vikalpanaiḥ ||

17.14
avipraṇāśā caiva syāt karma syād yadi saṃsavat |
yasmāt saṃsravaṇādeṣa vipraṇāso na vidyate ||

17.15
karma nāśena naivāsyā vipraṇāśo 'pi vidyate |
karmaṇāṃ cāvipraṇāśo dharmo yo buddhabhāṣitaḥ ||

17.16
sa cāpi deśito dharmaḥ karmāṇām ātmapāṭhakaḥ |
tasmān na karmāṇi kṛtvā vinaśyantīti cocyate ||

17.17
tatra yaḥ pratipakṣaḥ syād vyapadeśo na tatra saḥ |
prahāṇaṃ sa tu karmaṇāṃ yatrāsau tatra mucyate ||

17.18
karmaṇaḥ kārakaḥ sṛṣṭo nāsti sraṣṭā kuto 'sti ca |
yasmād dhātuḥ samutpanno na cāsti sa ca vidyate ||

17.19
vikurvaṇā kṛtā yena kartrā sā tu vikurvaṇā |
tathaiva kartrā yat karma kṛtaṃ tac ca vikurvaṇā ||

17.20
yadi svabhāvataḥ karma syān na sṛjyeta śāśvatam |
akṛtaṃ cābhyupetaṃ syāt karma duḥkham ca śāśvatam ||

17.21
karoti yadi vā karma karoty anubhavaty api |
anyena kṛtaṃ karma anyo 'nubhavatīti ca ||

17.22
priyam apriyam eṣaiva karmaṇaḥ phalam isyate |
saṃskārāṇām anādīnāṃ vyavahāro na sidhyati ||

17.23
na saṃbhavaḥ svabhāvasya yuktaḥ pratyayahetubhiḥ |
hetupratyayasaṃbhūtaḥ svabhāvaḥ kṛtako bhavet ||

17.24
kleśāś ca karmaṇaś caiva dehe santi na dehinām |
dehāś ca karmaṇaḥ kleśā yasmāt santi na dehinām ||

17.25
kleśāś ca karmaṇaś kāyāḥ kartāraś ca phalāni ca |
gandharvanagarākārā marīcisvapnasaṃnibhāḥ ||

17.26
kleśāś karma ca dehāś ca kartāraś ca phalāni ca |
gandharvanagarākārā marīcisvapnasaṃnibhāḥ ||

17.27
na saṃsāram utpādayanti na ca karma samudbhavanti |
na cāpi karmaṇā kleśāḥ kutaḥ karma bhaviṣyati ||

17.28
avidyā nisprakāśasya kasmād dhi parikalpyate |
tasmin satyāṃ pravartante kleśāś ca karma ca ||

17.29
avidyā yadi naivāsti karma cāpi na vidyate |
tasmāt karmāṇi kartāraṃ phalaṃ ca na vikalpayet ||

17.30
yathā nirmitakaṃ śāstā nirmimīta maharddhikaḥ |
tathā nirmitakas tasya nirmimītāparaṃ punaḥ ||

17.31
tathā kartā kṛtaṃ karma yan nirmitakavat kṛtam |
yathā nirmitakenānyo nirmitas tat tatheti ca ||

17.32
kleśāḥ karmāṇi dehāś ca kartāraś ca phalāni ca |
gandharvanagarākārā marīcisvapnasaṃnibhāḥ ||

17.33
kleśāḥ karma ca dehāś ca kartāraś ca phalāni ca |
gandharvanagarākārā marīcisvapnasaṃnibhāḥ ||
