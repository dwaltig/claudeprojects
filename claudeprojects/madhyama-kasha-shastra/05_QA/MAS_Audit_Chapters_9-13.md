## Chapter-by-Chapter Issues

### Chapter 9 (Pūrva-parīkṣā)
**Issues**:
- v9.1: *Darśana-śravaṇa-ādīni* glossed as "seeing/hearing" without noting these are *karaṇa* (instruments)
- v9.3: *Parigṛhṇīyād* (appropriation) lost in Blues version
- v9.7-9: Combined analysis loses individual verse arguments

**Recommendations**:
- Add footnote on *karaṇa-kartṛ* (instrument-agent) relation
- Separate v9.7-9 analysis when possible

### Chapter 10 (Agnīndhana-parīkṣā)
**Issues**:
- v10.6: *Strī/Puruṣa* analogy needs clearer rejection
- v10.15: *Ghaṭa-paṭa* reference excellent but could link to MMK Ch 5

**Recommendations**:
- Add cross-reference to Ch 5 (Dhātu) for pot/cloth analysis
- Clarify why meeting ≠ burning at v10.6

### Chapter 11 (Pūrvāpara-koṭi-parīkṣā)  
**Issues**:
- v11.1: *Anavarāgra* (beginningless) needs explicit connection to *pratītyasamutpāda*
- v11.2: "Middle" (*madhya*) without edges—temporal logic under-explained
- v11.7: *Atideśa* (extension) principle not flagged

**Recommendations**:
- Add note: "Beginninglessness is a corollary of dependent origination"
- Explicitly label the *atideśa* move in v11.7

### Chapter 12 (Duḥkha-parīkṣā)
**Issues**:
- v12.4: *Pudgala* used without distinguishing from Pudgalavādin "person" vs. *ātman*
- v12.1: *Catuṣkoṭi* not explicitly labeled as "tetralemma"

**Recommendations**:
- Add glossary entry distinguishing *pudgala* from *ātman*
- Label the Four Corners explicitly in commentary

### Chapter 13 (Saṃskāra-parīkṣā)
**Issues**:
- v13.1: *Moṣadharma* well-handled
- v13.7: Risk of implying emptiness is a "thing"
- Diacritic: "shunyata" found in one instance (should be *śūnyatā*)

**Recommendations**:
- Standardize diacritics throughout
- Add note: "Emptiness itself is empty of inherent existence"

---

## Overall Assessment

**Strengths**:
- Engaging Blues vernacular increases accessibility
- Captures rhetorical rhythm of Nāgārjuna's *prasaṅga* arguments
- Sanskrit source text included for all verses
- Philological commentary is substantive

**Areas for Improvement**:
1. **Terminological Precision**: Key terms sometimes replaced with metaphors
2. **Cross-References**: Need more links between chapters (Ch 10→Ch 5, Ch 11→Ch 1)
3. **Doctrinal Clarity**: Add notes to prevent emptiness reification

**Final Verdict**: Pass with Minor Revisions
