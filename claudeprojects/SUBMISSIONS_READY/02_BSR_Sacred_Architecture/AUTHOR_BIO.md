# Author Bio - William Altig

William Altig is an independent scholar based in Houston, TX, specializing in Buddhist Studies and Digital Humanities. His current research focuses on the intersection of sacred text translation, audio production methodology, and AI-assisted textual scholarship. He has completed translations of the Lotus Sutra and Vimalakirti Sutra, with emphasis on making classical Buddhist texts accessible through both scholarly and vernacular idioms.
