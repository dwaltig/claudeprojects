# Author Bio - William Altig

William Altig is an independent scholar specializing in comparative studies of Buddhist philosophy and consciousness science. His current research focuses on structural parallels between classical Mahayana Buddhist texts and contemporary theories of mind, particularly Integrated Information Theory. He has completed translations of the Lotus Sutra and Vimalakirti Sutra, with emphasis on bridging technical Buddhist philosophy and accessible contemporary idiom. His work explores the intersection of contemplative phenomenology, philosophy of mind, and formal theories of consciousness.
