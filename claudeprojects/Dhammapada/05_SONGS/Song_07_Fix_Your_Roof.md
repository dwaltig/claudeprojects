## Notes on the Blues

This song channels the deep American anxiety of housing insecurity‚Äîthe fear of the roof caving in, literally and metaphorically. From the Blues tradition of "Trouble in Mind" to contemporary precarity, we know what it feels like to be one bad week away from disaster.

**Contemporary resonance:**
- Housing crisis and the working poor
- Mental health and emotional flooding
- The difference between those who crumble in crisis and those who stay grounded
- Therapy, meditation, and spiritual practice as preventive maintenance
- Climate anxiety‚Äîliteral storms getting worse
- Being unprepared for life's inevitable shocks

The Blues has always been sung by people who KNOW what a leaky roof feels like. This song honors that while pointing toward the inner work that makes the difference.
