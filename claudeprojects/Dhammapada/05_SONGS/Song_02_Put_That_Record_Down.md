## The Teaching
Verses 3-4 warn about looping grievances: keep replaying "he abused me, he robbed me" and hate keeps burning; set the record down and the fire cools. The choice is in what you rehearse.

## Suno Production Brief (Marcus "Groove" Washington)
**Emotion:** Release from rumination; weary-to-light.
**Tempo/Feel:** 72 BPM slow shuffle, late-night Chicago bar, space between phrases.
**Palette:** Warm Gibson through a tube amp, brushed snare, upright bass walk, Hammond B3 pad, subtle tremolo guitar answering the vocal, optional muted trumpet on bridge.
**Vocal:** Weathered, intimate; verse phrasing behind the beat, chorus a touch stronger. Spoken drop on "Put that record down" before final chorus works.
