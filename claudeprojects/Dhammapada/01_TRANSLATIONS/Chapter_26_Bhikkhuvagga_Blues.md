### Verse 370: The Rule of Fives

**The Blues Truth:**

CUT five.  
ABANDON five.  
CULTIVATE five more.

The monk who surmounts five attachments  
Is called a FLOOD-CROSSER.

What are these fives?  
**Cut**: the five lower fetters (identity view, doubt, rites-and-rituals clinging, sensual desire, ill-will).  
**Abandon**: the five higher fetters (craving for form, craving for formless, conceit, restlessness, ignorance).  
**Cultivate**: the five spiritual faculties (faith, energy, mindfulness, concentration, wisdom).  
**Surmount**: the five attachments (passion, hatred, delusion, conceit, wrong view).

Cross the flood.
