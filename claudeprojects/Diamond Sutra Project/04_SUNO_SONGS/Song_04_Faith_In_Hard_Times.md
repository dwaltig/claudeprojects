## Musical Recommendations

### Primary Sound Palette
Contemplative minor-key blues that opens into major on the faith chorus. The "Got nothing, gave nothing" section should feel like a breakthrough moment.

### Tempo & Feel
68-72 BPM throughout. Slow and spacious. Let the questions breathe.

### Instrumentation
- Acoustic guitar (verses)
- Hammond B3 organ (swells on choruses)
- Electric guitar (enters on "Got nothing")
- Minimal drums‚Äîbrushes, minimal kit

### Vocal Direction
Vulnerable on the questions. Reassuring on Buddha's answers. Triumphant-in-paradox on "Got nothing, gave nothing, received everything."
