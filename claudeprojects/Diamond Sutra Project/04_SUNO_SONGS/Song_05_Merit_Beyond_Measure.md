## Musical Recommendations

### Primary Sound Palette
Epic blues suite‚Äîthis is the climax of the Foundations section. Three movements that build to the central teaching: "Abide nowhere."

### Tempo & Feel
- Part 1 (Treasures/Four Lines): 75 BPM, building energy
- Part 2 (Four Stages): 70 BPM, walking rhythm
- Part 3 (Abide Nowhere): 65 BPM, spacious and resolving

### Instrumentation
- Full band throughout
- Hammond B3 organ (foundation)
- Electric guitar (melodic lines)
- Horns on choruses (Memphis style)
- Choir backing on "Abide nowhere"

### Vocal Direction
Testimony style throughout. Building conviction on "Four lines." Paradoxical wisdom on "Giving up climbing." Peaceful certainty on "Abide nowhere."
