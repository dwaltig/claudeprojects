### The Professor's Scholarly Review

**Strengths**: Patel nailed the "space immeasurability" teaching. That's the core move‚Äîwhen you stop measuring, it becomes infinite. He also got the "marks/no-marks" paradox right. This ain't about destroying the Buddha's body; it's about seeing *through* form to formlessness.
