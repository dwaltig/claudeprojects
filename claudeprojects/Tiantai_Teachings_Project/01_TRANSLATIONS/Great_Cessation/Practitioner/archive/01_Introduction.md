### Practitioner's Note:
Zhiyi didn't finish the book. He stopped at "Views." That means the rest is up to us. The text isn't a dead thing; it's a broken-off sentence. We are the punctuation. When he asks if this wisdom is "innate" or "learned," he's asking: *Is this jazz just something you feel, or did you learn your scales?* The answer, of course, is both.
