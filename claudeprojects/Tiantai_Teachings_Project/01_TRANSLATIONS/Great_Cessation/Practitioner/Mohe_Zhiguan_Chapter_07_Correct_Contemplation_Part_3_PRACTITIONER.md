# Mohe Zhiguan - Chapter 7: The Sledgehammer (Universal Breaking) - The Practitioner's Blues

## The Practitioner's Review
"This is the violent part.
Master Zhiyi hands you a sledgehammer (Diamond Wisdom) and tells you to smash everything in your house.
Not just the trash (Bad Habits). He wants you to smash the furniture, the windows, and even the family photos (Good Deeds, Meditation, Insights).
Why? Because you're 'Stuck.' And if you're stuck on it, it's a trap."

### 1. The Four Walls (The Four Gates)
"Every idea you have is a room with four walls. To get out, you have to break one of them down.
1.  **The 'It's Real' Wall:** You think your problem is solid. (Smash it: It's empty).
2.  **The 'It's Nothing' Wall:** You think nothing matters. (Smash it: It matters, just not how you think).
3.  **The 'It's Both' Wall:** You try to compromise. (Smash it: That's just confusion).
4.  **The 'It's Neither' Wall:** You try to be clever and say 'It's mysterious.' (Smash it: That's just hiding)."

### 2. The Four Questions (The Tetralemma)
"Pick something you believe in. Maybe 'I am smart.' Ask:
1.  **Did I create this smarts?** (No, I was born ignorant).
2.  **Did the books create it?** (No, books can't think).
3.  **Did we do it together?** (Two wrongs don't make a right).
4.  **Did it happen by accident?** (No, I studied).
**Result:** 'Smart' is just a word. Smash it."

### 3. Smashing the Good Stuff
"This is the hard part.
It's easy to smash 'Anger.' Who wants that?
But Master Zhiyi says you gotta smash 'Peace,' 'Joy,' and 'Holiness' too.
*   If you sit in meditation and feel great, and you say 'I love this,' **Smash it.** You're stuck in the feeling.
*   If you have a great insight and say 'I'm a genius,' **Smash it.** You're stuck in the ego.
*   If you see the Buddha, **Smash him.** (Not the real Buddha, but the *picture* of him in your head).

**The Freedom:**
When you break the chains of 'Bad,' you're halfway there.
When you break the chains of 'Good,' you're Free.
You can still *do* good, but you aren't *trapped* by it."
