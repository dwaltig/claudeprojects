# Mohe Zhiguan - Chapter 5: The Real Deal (Biased vs. Perfect) - The Practitioner's Blues

## The Practitioner's Review
"We've covered a lot of ground. But Master Zhiyi has one more warning before we start the actual work.
He says: **'Don't buy the knockoffs.'**
There are a lot of versions of Buddhism out there. Some are 'Small,' some are 'Half-finished,' some are 'Biased.'
You want the **Full, Perfect, Sudden, Real Deal.**"

### 1. Small vs. Large (The Toy Car vs. The Bus)
*   **Small:** You're just trying to save yourself. You see the world as a problem to be smashed (Analyzed). You're driving a toy car; there's only room for you.
*   **Large:** You realize the world isn't a problem; it's a dream. You don't have to smash a dream; you just wake up. And you wake up everyone else too. You're driving the Big Bus.

### 2. Half vs. Full (The Snack vs. The Feast)
*   **Half:** You get some truth, but not all of it. It stops your hunger, but it doesn't make you strong.
*   **Full:** You get the whole menu. Nothing left out.

### 3. Biased vs. Perfect (The Limp vs. The Stride)
*   **Biased:** You lean too much on 'Emptiness' (escaping the world) or too much on 'Distinction' (analyzing the world). You walk with a limp.
*   **Perfect:** You walk straight down the Middle. You see that the Empty *is* the World, and the World *is* the Empty. No limp.

### 4. Gradual vs. Sudden (The Stairs vs. The Elevator)
*   **Gradual:** You climb one step at a time. First you learn A, then B, then C. It takes eons.
*   **Sudden:** You press the button and go straight to the Penthouse. You realize that 'A' was 'Z' all along. The milk *is* the Ghee.

### 5. Expedient vs. Real (The Magic Trick vs. The Gold)
*   **Expedient:** The Buddha used tricks to get us to listen. He talked about a 'Magic City' to let us rest.
*   **Real:** He reveals the Treasure. The Magic City disappears. You realize you were standing on the Gold the whole time.

1.  **Check your Motivation:** Are you just trying to escape your own pain? (Small). Or are you trying to wake up for everyone? (Large).
2.  **Check your View:** Do you think 'Real Life' is over there, and 'Spiritual Life' is over here? (Biased). Or do you see them as one? (Perfect).
3.  **Check your Speed:** Are you waiting for 'Someday' to be enlightened? (Gradual). Or are you willing to see the Buddha in your mirror right now? (Sudden).
