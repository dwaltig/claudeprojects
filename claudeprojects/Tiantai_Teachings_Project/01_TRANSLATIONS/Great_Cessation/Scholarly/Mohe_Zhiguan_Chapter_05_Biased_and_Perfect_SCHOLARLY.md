## üìú Doctrinal Analysis

1.  **The Five Pairs:** This is a comprehensive grid for evaluating any Buddhist text or practice. It prevents the practitioner from getting stuck in a "lesser" vehicle while thinking it is the ultimate.
2.  **The "Separate" as Biased:** This is Tiantai's unique critique. Even the "Separate" Teaching (Mahayana which teaches Emptiness separately from Existence) is considered "Biased" because it sets up a duality between Samsara and Nirvana. Only the "Perfect" Teaching (Samsara *is* Nirvana) is truly Non-Dual.
3.  **The "Sudden" Cow:** The metaphor of the cow eating *K·π£ƒÅnti* grass and immediately producing Ghee (the final product of milk processing) illustrates the immediacy of the Perfect Teaching. In other paths, you must process the milk (practice) through many stages (cream, butter) to get Ghee (Enlightenment). In the Perfect Path, the milk *is* the Ghee.
