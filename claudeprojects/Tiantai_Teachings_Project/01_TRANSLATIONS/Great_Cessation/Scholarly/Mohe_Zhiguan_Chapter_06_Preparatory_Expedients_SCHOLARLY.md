1.  **The "Far" Expedients:** Why are they called "Far"? Because they are the *external* preparation. The "Near" expedients (Ten Objects) are the *internal* mental work.
2.  **The Art of Adjustment:** Section 4 (Adjusting Five Matters) is one of the most practical guides in Buddhist literature. It treats meditation not just as a mental act but as a physiological one. The distinction between Wind, Gasp, Qi, and Breath (*XÄ«*) is a classic Tiantai diagnostic tool for breath meditation.
