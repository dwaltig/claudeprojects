1.  **Ti-Zhen (Substantializing the Real):** Unlike the "Analytical" (*XÄ«*) emptiness of the Hinayana (breaking a cup to see it's not a cup), this is "Substantial" emptiness (seeing the cup *is* empty right now). This is the hallmark of Mahayana emptiness.
2.  **Entering the Provisional:** This is the unique Bodhisattva path. The Arhat enters Emptiness and stays there (Nirvana). The Bodhisattva enters Emptiness, realizes it's just a state, and *comes back* to the Provisional world to work, but without attachment. This "Coming Back" is the *Cong Kong Ru Jia*.
3.  **The Middle Way:** The integration. It's not a third place. It's realizing that the Empty (Absolute) and the Provisional (Relative) are the same thing. Samsara *is* Nirvana. This is the **Perfect Substance**.
