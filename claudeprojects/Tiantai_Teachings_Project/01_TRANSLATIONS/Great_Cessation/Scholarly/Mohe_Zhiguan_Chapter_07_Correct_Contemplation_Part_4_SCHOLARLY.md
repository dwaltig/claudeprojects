## ğŸ“œ Doctrinal Analysis

1.  **Passage and Blockage:** This is the feedback loop of meditation. It is not enough to just "meditate"; one must constantly evaluate the *quality* of the meditation.
2.  **Dao-Pin (37 Factors):** Zhiyi integrates the classic Hinayana list of 37 factors into the Mahayana path, treating them as "functional adjustments" for the mind rather than just doctrinal categories.
3.  **No Dharma Love:** This is critical. The greatest enemy of Enlightenment is not evil; it is the *attachment to Good*. The "Similitude Stage" (resembling enlightenment) is the most dangerous place because it feels like the goal. One must step off the 100-foot pole.
