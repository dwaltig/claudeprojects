1.  **True Bodhicitta:** In Tiantai, true Bodhicitta is not just a good intention; it is an ontological realization. It arises *from* the insight into the Inconceivable. You save beings because you see they are *already* the Buddha-nature, but they don't know it.
2.  **An Xin (Resting the Mind):** This is the bridge between the Vow (Mode 2) and the Breaking (Mode 4). Before you start smashing delusions, you must have a stable base. "Resting" means finding the "Middle" where the mind is neither tense nor lax.
