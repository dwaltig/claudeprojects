## Detailed Fascicle Mapping

### 卷一 Fascicle 1
*   **Preface序:** Fayun's preface; 妙法 (Subtle Dharma) doctrine ✅
*   **經題釋:** Title exegesis: 妙 (Miao), Cause-Fruit (因果), Lotus metaphor ✅
*   **通序:** Universal preface (5 elements) ✅
*   **別序:** Six Portents (六瑞) & special preface ✅
*   **疑念序:** Maitreya's doubt & Mañjuśrī's response ✅

### 卷二 Fascicle 2
*   **答問序:** Mañjuśrī's answer: Sun-Moon-Lamp Buddha precedent ✅
*   **方便品:** Skillful Means: **Two Wisdoms (二智)**, Ten Suchnesses (十如是) ✅
*   **三請:** Three Requests, 5000 arrogant departures ✅

### 卷三 Fascicle 3
*   **法說段:** Dharma-explanation stage: The Four Unities (Unity of Teaching, Person, Cause, Fruit) ✅
*   **譬喻品:** Parable of the Burning House: **Four Vehicles debate (四車)** ✅

### 卷四 Fascicle 4
*   **譬喻品餘:** Burning House: Interpretation of Ten Metaphors ✅
*   **信解品:** Parable of the Poor Son: **Dung/Wages doctrine** ✅

### 卷五 Fascicle 5
*   **信解品餘:** Poor Son continued: Father-Son reunion allegory ✅
*   **藥草喻品:** Medicinal Herbs: **Capacity ranks (草木五品)** ✅

### 卷六 Fascicle 6
*   **授記品:** Prophecies for Disciples: **Buddha-Name doctrine** ✅

### 卷七 Fascicle 7
*   **化城喻品:** Phantom City: **500 Yojanas allegory**, Rest-Stop doctrine ✅
*   **五百弟子授記品:** Prophecy for the mass of disciples ✅
*   **授學無學人記品:** Prophecy for Ananda and Rahula ✅
*   **見寶塔品:** Beholding the Jeweled Stupa: **Non-Duality of Trace and Origin** ✅

### 卷八 Fascicle 8
*   **安樂行品:** Peaceful Practices ✅
*   **從地涌出品:** Bodhisattvas from the Earth: **The Original Retinue** ✅
*   **壽量品:** Lifespan of the Tathāgata: **Long-but-finite doctrine** ✅
*   **流通分:** Circulation section: Transmission by Merit and by Example ✅
