# Tiantai Teachings Project Glossary

## Hybrid Scholarly/Practitioner Terminology

This glossary bridges the scholarly (The Architect) and practitioner (The Practitioner) editions of the Fahua Xuanyi translation project.

---

## Core Tiantai Doctrines

| Chinese | Pinyin | Sanskrit | Scholarly | Practitioner | Definition |
|---------|--------|----------|-----------|--------------|------------|
| 三諦 | sān dì | — | Three Truths | Three Ways of Seeing | Empty, Provisional, Middle—the cornerstone of Tiantai philosophy |
| 三觀 | sān guān | — | Three Contemplations | Three Looks | Meditation on the Three Truths simultaneously |
| 一念三千 | yīniàn sānqiān | — | 3,000 Realms in One Thought-Moment | Every Moment Contains Everything | The capstone of Tiantai metaphysics |
| 圓融 | yuán róng | — | Perfect Interfusion | Already-One | Non-dual integration of all phenomena |
| 十妙 | shí miào | — | Ten Subtleties | Ten Wonders | Framework for analyzing "wonderful" |
| 十界 | shí jiè | — | Ten Dharma-Realms | Ten Worlds | Hell through Buddha—complete taxonomy of existence |
| 十如 | shí rú | — | Ten Suchnesses | Ten Ways Things Are | Appearance, Nature, Substance, Power, Function, Causes, Conditions, Effects, Retributions, Ultimate Equality |
| 六即 | liù jí | — | Six Stages of Identity | Six Steps to Knowing Who You Are | From principle to realization |
