### Humanization Recommendations

**Priority 1 (Must-Fix):**
1. **Inject Narrative** — Start Introduction with specific translation problem: "When translating the Diamond Sutra's line 'all conditioned phenomena are like a dream,' our GPT-4 renderings consistently lost the metaphor's tactile quality."
2. **Vary Architecture** — Break uniform 5-line paragraphs; use 1-line claims + longer explanations
3. **Own the Argument** — Replace "It may be suggested" with "I contend that..." Remove 30% of hedging modals

**Priority 2 (Recommended):**
4. **Personalize Transitions** — Swap "Furthermore" for "This technical challenge is deepened by a theological one:"
5. **Show the Work** — Include messy human detail: "After three unsatisfactory ensemble outputs, we introduced a rule-based post-processor—a solution drawn from a translator's offhand comment."
6. **Disrupt Rhythm** — Use mid-sentence em-dashes, vary sentence length dramatically

**Priority 3 (Optional):**
7. **Contract Selectively** — Use 1-2 contractions in conclusion ("can't ignore," "it's evident")
