# Track 5: Living Waters
## Chapter 10: The Teacher of the Law

### Description
A blues-folk meditation on the exhaustion of trying to earn your worth through endless striving. This song walks through the valley of good intentions gone sour, calloused hands and hollow prayers, until a stranger by the roadside offers an ancient truth: the light you've been climbing toward was inside you all along. Inspired by the "Digging for Water" parable.
