# Track 3: The Hidden Jewel
## Chapter 4 & 8: Belief and Understanding

### Description
Based on the Parable of the Jewel in the Robe. This song tells the story of a man living in poverty while carrying a priceless gem sewn into his cloak by a concerned friend. A metaphor for the inherent Buddha-nature within all beings.
