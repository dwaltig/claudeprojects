	2.	Enhanced Lighting: We’ll look into improving street and area lighting for better visibility at night, which is a fundamental deterrent to criminal activities.  
	3.	Surveillance Systems: Implementing surveillance cameras at strategic points, especially near entryways, will help us monitor the comings and goings within the community. This measure is aimed at increasing safety while respecting the privacy of our residents.  
	4.	Hiring Security Personnel: Stationing trained security officers at the entrance and patrolling the community could significantly enhance our preventive measures against potential criminal activities.  
	5.	Visitor Logs: Keeping a log of all non-residents entering Briarpoint will help us monitor activity and could be crucial in the event of any incident.  
	6.	Community Engagement: Encouraging a close-knit community where residents are familiar with each other and are proactive in reporting suspicious activities. This approach not only strengthens security but also fosters a sense of belonging and collective responsibility among residents.  
  
I believe these measures, while not implying guaranteed security, will significantly contribute to the safety and well-being of our community. I look forward to discussing these proposals further and finding a solution that benefits all parties involved.
