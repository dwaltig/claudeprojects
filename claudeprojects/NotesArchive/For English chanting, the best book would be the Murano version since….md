For English chanting, the best book would be the Murano version since it is the one used in Nichiren Shu. However, alternatives such as the one by Gene Reeves and the Kato version [the Threefold Lotus Sutra] are acceptable. For Shindokou, go to lulu.com https://www.lulu.com/en/us/shop/ryuoh-michael-faulconer/myoho-renge-kyo-romanized/paperback/product-159gjn6j.html You may contact NBIC in Hayward to see if they have the Lotus Sutra CDs available.
