# Chant of the Unyielding Protector  
  
*“Chant of the Unyielding Protector”* is a stirring Gregorian chant inspired by the vows of Bodhisattva Pradanasura in the Lotus Sutra. This sacred piece calls upon powerful protective spells to safeguard those who uphold and share the teachings of the Dharma. With its steady rhythm and solemn melody, the chant creates a shield of resilience and spiritual fortitude, empowering listeners to rise above challenges and fears.  
  
**Purport**  
  
This chant reflects the unwavering compassion of Bodhisattva Pradanasura, who vows to protect practitioners of the Lotus Sutra from all forms of harm. The sacred spells encapsulated in this piece are talismans of spiritual strength, courage, and perseverance. Listening to this chant evokes the presence of a guardian who shields the devoted, encouraging them to remain steadfast in their path of wisdom and compassion.

**Antiphon:**  
*We offer spells of holy might,*  
*Guardians of those who preach the light.*  
  
**Verses:**  
In gvale and mahāgvale, strength is found,  
Ukke mukke, courage unbound.  
Ade adāvati, stand firm, unstopped,  
Tritye trityāvati, rise, unopposed atop.  
  
**Chorus:**  
*By these spells, no harm shall fall,*  
*Sacred words to shield them all.*  
  
Itini, vitini, kitini, spirit flies,  
Tritti trityāvati svāhā, no fear, no ties.  
By countless Buddhas these words are sealed,  
A shield of wisdom, their fate revealed.
