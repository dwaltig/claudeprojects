**Song Description:**  
  
"Noble Ones of the Earth" is a soulful folk song inspired by Chapter Fifteen of the Lotus Sutra. The song celebrates the awakening of the "Noble Ones" within each of us and our capacity to embody enlightenment and compassion. With powerful choir voices, acoustic guitar, piano, and a gentle rhythm, the lyrics reflect a journey of inner awakening and a call to serve and protect the Dharma. The song's message is universal, encouraging listeners to recognize their innate wisdom and the call of the Enlightened One in every heart.
