# Noble Eightfold Path  
  
Right view  
Actions have consequences  
I am the King of the world  
I am awakening  
Appearance  
  
Right resolve  
Make a determination to live your life in accord with your right views i.e. truth  
Make a Vow!  
Align your awareness to this new insight  
Nature  
  
Right speech  
Speak appropriately  
What comes out reflects what’s inside  
Entity  
  
Right action  
Act fulfilling the meaning of your new insight  
Act with the wisdom “Actions have consequences”  
Power  
  
Right livelihood  
Surround yourself with proof of your actions  
Influence  
  
Right effort  
Generate wholesome thoughts cut off unwholesome thoughts.   
Internal cause  
  
Right mindfulness  
Reflect on your insights, efforts, growing body of truth.   
Learn. Grow.   
Relation  
  
Right Samadhi  
Through our practice  
Meditation and mindfulness this Equanimous￼￼ Awakened present awareness blossoms. Like a Lotus blossom offers it seeds.  
Bringing insight.  
  
This Latent potential leads to Manifest Effect  
  
Remain focused   
Consistent from beginning to end

Faith in the Buddha’s teachings  
Four Noble Truths and the Eightfold Path
