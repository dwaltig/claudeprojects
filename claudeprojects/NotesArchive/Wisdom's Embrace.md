# Wisdom's Embrace  
  
In hearts where shadows dwell, a foolish thought takes hold,   
In hearts where shadows dwell, a foolish thought takes hold,   
"No God above," they cry, in arrogance untold.   
"No God above," they cry, in arrogance untold.   
Corruption's stain spreads wide, a world in disarray,   
Corruption's stain spreads wide, a world in disarray,   
No goodness shines within, as darkness clouds the day.  
No goodness shines within, as darkness clouds the day.  
  
From heavens high, the LORD observes this mortal plight,   
From heavens high, the LORD observes this mortal plight,   
Seeking wisdom's gleam, a soul yearning for light.   
Seeking wisdom's gleam, a soul yearning for light.   
But faithless hearts abound, a tapestry of wrong,   
But faithless hearts abound, a tapestry of wrong,   
No virtuous deed is found, the wicked's reign prolongs.  
No virtuous deed is found, the wicked's reign prolongs.  
  
Do they not comprehend, these souls consumed by greed,   
Do they not comprehend, these souls consumed by greed,   
Who feast upon the weak, their cries unheard, unheed?   
Who feast upon the weak, their cries unheard, unheed?   
Yet fear will grip their hearts, a tremor deep inside,   
Yet fear will grip their hearts, a tremor deep inside,   
For God stands with the just, their refuge and their guide.  
For God stands with the just, their refuge and their guide.  
  
Though troubles may beset, and shadows dim the way,   
Though troubles may beset, and shadows dim the way,   
The LORD remains a shield, a fortress come what may.   
The LORD remains a shield, a fortress come what may.   
The wicked's schemes will fail, their plans to dust consigned,   
The wicked's schemes will fail, their plans to dust consigned,   
For in the heart of faith, true solace we shall find.  
For in the heart of faith, true solace we shall find.  
  
This timeless truth unfolds, in sutra's wisdom bright,   
This timeless truth unfolds, in sutra's wisdom bright,   
A Buddha-nature sleeps, in every soul alight.   
A Buddha-nature sleeps, in every soul alight.   
Though ignorance may veil, compassion's flame endures,   
Though ignorance may veil, compassion's flame endures,   
A boundless potential waits, for awakening is yours.  
A boundless potential waits, for awakening is yours.  
  
So let us cast aside the darkness of despair,   
So let us cast aside the darkness of despair,   
Embrace the Lotus bloom, its petals pure and fair.   
Embrace the Lotus bloom, its petals pure and fair.   
In every heart, a seed of Buddhahood resides,   
In every heart, a seed of Buddhahood resides,   
A path to liberation, where love and wisdom guides.
