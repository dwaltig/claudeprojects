  
# Light of Truth  
  
“Light of Truth” is a heartfelt blues folk song inspired by themes of resilience, authenticity, and the journey toward inner peace. With soul-stirring lyrics and a blend of acoustic slide guitar, fiddle, and upright bass, this song captures the essence of finding beauty and strength in life’s trials. Its rich, melodic tones and introspective storytelling invite listeners to reflect on their own paths of growth and understanding.
