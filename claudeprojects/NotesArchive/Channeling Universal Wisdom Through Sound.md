**Channeling Universal Wisdom Through Sound**  
  
Songs by The William Altig Choir are rooted in spiritual texts like the *Lotus Sutra* carry a foundation of universal truth. Music allows for us to express the essence of these teachings in ways words alone cannot. These vibrations of melody, rhythm, and harmony engage listeners on a deep emotional level, bypassing intellectual filters and touching our soul directly.  
  
**How:** Incorporating tonalities and scales that evoke a sense of awe and introspection. For example, minor scales or modes like the Dorian or Phrygian can convey mystery and transcendence, while pentatonic scales suggest simplicity and universality.
