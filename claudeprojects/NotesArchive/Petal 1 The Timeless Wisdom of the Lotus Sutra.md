Petal 1: The Timeless Wisdom of the Lotus Sutra  
   
Timeless Lotus Sutra,  
Resonates through ages past,  
Guide to awakening.  
   
Petal 2: An Extraordinary Revelation  
   
Sage Buddha's insight,  
Lotus Sutra lights the path,  
To love and peace found.  
   
Petal 3: Embracing the Eternal Truth  
   
Sacred verses speak,  
Gods and men in dance entwined,  
Truths of existence.  
   
Petal 4: Mystical Parables and Cosmic Narratives  
   
Stories deep and vast,  
Hidden wisdom of Buddha,  
Enlightenment calls.  
   
Petal 5: The Cosmic Dance of Buddhas  
   
Dance of Buddhas bright,  
From the vast expanse they come,  
Guide us to the light.  
   
Petal 6: The Lotus of Infinite Potential  
   
Buddha-nature shines,  
In all beings it does dwell,  
Awaiting its bloom.  
   
Petal 7: Empowering the Aspirant  
   
Verse of empowerment,  
Shackles break, ego fades away,  
Awakening calls.  
   
Petal 8: Universal Wisdom for All  
   
Transcending boundaries,  
The Lotus Sutra speaks to all,  
In unity bound.  
   
Petal 9: Beyond the World of Limitation  
   
Beyond limitations,  
Compassion’s call beckons us,  
To liberation.  
   
Petal 10: Embracing the Eternal Dharma  
   
Impermanence fades,  
Lotus Sutra's truth remains,  
In eternal flow.  
  
  
Lotus Sutra speaks,    
Karma, mind weave life's fabric—    
Freedom in insight.
