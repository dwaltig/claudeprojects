# Understand By Faith  
  
“Understand by Faith” is a soulful, contemplative song inspired by the teachings of the Enlightened One. It explores the harmonious relationship between faith and understanding, emphasizing that true wisdom comes not just from intellectual comprehension but from trusting one’s inner light. The song invites listeners to open their hearts, let go of doubts, and embrace the deeper truths that guide us on our spiritual journey.
