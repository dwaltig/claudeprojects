# Radiant Light  
#   
*Preserve us, Lord, hear our silent plea,*  
*Preserve us, Lord, hear our silent plea,*  
*A yearning heart in search of sanctuary.*  
*A yearning heart in search of sanctuary.*  
*Yet shadows dance, doubts veil our sight,*  
*Yet shadows dance, doubts veil our sight,*  
*Can inner light bring true respite?*  
*Can inner light bring true respite?*  
  
*Within each soul, a light resides,*  
*Within each soul, a light resides,*  
*A steadfast strength that gently guides.*  
*A steadfast strength that gently guides.*  
*Though shadows fall and doubts obscure,*  
*Though shadows fall and doubts obscure,*  
*Our inner truth shall still endure.*  
*Our inner truth shall still endure.*  
*Our inner truth shall still endure.*  
*Our inner truth shall still endure.*  
*Our inner truth shall still endure.*  
  
*Through trials faced and battles won,*  
*Through trials faced and battles won,*  
*The spirit’s strength shines like the sun.*  
*The spirit’s strength shines like the sun.*  
*In acts of love and boundless grace,*  
*In acts of love and boundless grace,*  
*We glimpse the truth of a radiant face.*  
*We glimpse the truth of a radiant face.*  
  
*In smiles of kindness freely given,*  
*In smiles of kindness freely given,*  
*In whispered prayers to skies unhidden,*  
*In whispered prayers to skies unhidden,*  
*In every hand outstretched to heal,*  
*In every hand outstretched to heal,*  
*The sacred light begins to reveal.*  
*The sacred light begins to reveal.*  
  
*In quiet night, the heart’s soft beat,*  
*In quiet night, the heart’s soft beat,*  
*Whispers wisdom, calm and sweet.*  
*Whispers wisdom, calm and sweet.*  
*The inner light, a guiding ray,*  
*The inner light, a guiding ray,*  
*Dispels the shadows, lights our way.*  
*Dispels the shadows, lights our way.*  
  
*Beyond the grave, our hopes take flight,*  
*Beyond the grave, our hopes take flight,*  
*Embracing joy, a radiant light.*  
*Embracing joy, a radiant light.*  
*In trust we rest, our spirits free,*  
*In trust we rest, our spirits free,*  
*Bathed within love’s immensity.*
