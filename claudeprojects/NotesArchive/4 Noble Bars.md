# 4 Noble Bars  
  
**"4 Noble Bars"** is a modern Afrobeats track that blends rhythmic grooves with profound wisdom inspired by the Four Noble Truths of Buddhism. Through insightful lyrics and a compelling beat, this song explores the reality of suffering (dukkha), the causes of craving (tanha), and the path to liberation through the Eightfold Path.  
With verses full of vivid imagery and a catchy chorus, "4 Noble Bars" takes listeners on a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening, merging ancient truths with contemporary soundscapes.
