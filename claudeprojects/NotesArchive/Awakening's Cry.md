# Awakening's Cry  
  
This soulful blues track explores the depths of human struggle and the enduring hope for awakening, drawing inspiration from both Psalm 14 and the Lotus Sutra. Raw vocals and poignant lyrics intertwine with bluesy guitar riffs, creating a powerful meditation on compassion, wisdom, and the inherent Buddha-nature within all beings. "Lotus Blues" invites you on a journey from darkness to light, reminding us that even amidst life's challenges, liberation is possible for everyone.
