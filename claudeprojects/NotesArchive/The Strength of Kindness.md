# The Strength of Kindness  
  
Verse 1:  
The strength of kindness builds my home;   
forbearance clothes me tight,  
In abstraction's peaceful arms,   
I find the truth and light.  
When faced with clods, with sticks, or threats,   
remember me and stand,  
My spirit stretches far and wide,   
a guide through every land.  
  
Chorus:  
In whispers of the mountain air,   
in the echoes of the deep,  
My teachings flow through all who dare   
their sacred oath to keep.  
To the brave who walks this path with me,   
even when I'm gone,  
My creations come,   
a sight to see,   
ensuring he's never alone.
