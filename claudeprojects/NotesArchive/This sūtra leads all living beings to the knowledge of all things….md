*This sūtra leads all living beings to the knowledge of all things.*  
*This sūtra leads all living beings to the knowledge of all things.*  
  
The Buddha makes this declaration to Mañjuśrī Bodhisattva in Chapter Fourteen of the Lotus Sūtra. The Buddha shows us that it is possible to exist in harmony with the world rather than in conflict. It is only when we practice his teachings that we can believe them.
