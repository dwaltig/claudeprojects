# Canticle of Sacred Sanctuary  
  
*“Canticle of Sacred Sanctuary”* is a meditative Gregorian chant inspired by the protective spells recited in the Lotus Sutra. This piece invokes the sacred power of words to offer spiritual refuge and shield those who uphold and share the teachings. With its solemn tones and rhythmic chants, it creates a sanctuary of peace, resilience, and divine grace. Let the sacred vibrations of this canticle guide you into a space of courage and tranquility, protected by the blessings of countless Buddhas.  
  
**Purport**  
  
This canticle reflects the profound compassion of the Bodhisattvas and divine beings who pledge their protection to those upholding the Lotus Sutra. Each chant is an invocation of spiritual power, designed to fortify the soul against adversity, whether physical, mental, or spiritual. By listening to this piece, may you connect with the eternal sanctuary within and feel the protective embrace of the universal Law.

**Antiphon:**  
*For those who keep the Lotus Sūtra,*  
*We offer spells of safeguard and peace.*  
  
**Verses:**  
Anye manye, mane mamane,  
Kitte karite, samitāvi.  
Sānte, mukte, and muktatame,  
Same avishame, samasame.  
  
**Chorus:**  
*Sanctuary we bring,*  
*In sacred chants we sing.*  
  
Gaye, kshaye, fears erase,  
Akshine, sānte sanī, sacred space.  
Dhārani ālokabhāshe, shining light,  
Pratyavekshani, nidhini, in darkest night.  
  
**Chorus:**  
*Sanctuary we bring,*  
*In sacred chants we sing.*  
  
Abhyantaravisishte, utkule mutkule,  
Asade, parade, adversity flee.  
Sukānkshī, asamasame, find peace,  
Buddhavilokite, dharmaparikshite.  
  
**Chorus:**  
*Sanctuary we bring,*  
*In sacred chants we sing.*  
  
Sanghanirghoshani, nirghoshanī, raise thy voice,  
Bhayābhayasodhanī, make courage thy choice.  
Mantre mantrākshayate, rutakausalye,  
Sacred decree, in every melody.  
  
**Chorus:**  
*Sanctuary we bring,*  
*In sacred chants we sing.*  
  
Akshaye, akshavanataāya, unbreakable stay,  
Vakule valoda, firm in the way.  
Amanyatāya, in truth, stand tall,  
These sacred words protect us all.
