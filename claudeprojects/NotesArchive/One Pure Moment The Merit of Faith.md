# One Pure Moment: The Merit of Faith  
  
This gentle folk song tells the tale of Anya, a woman whose simple acts of kindness and unwavering faith outshine a lifetime of meritorious deeds. Inspired by a parable from the Lotus Sutra, the song reminds us that true enlightenment lies not in the quantity of our actions but in the quality of our connection to the Buddha's wisdom. With its soothing melody and thought-provoking lyrics, "One Pure Moment" invites listeners to reflect on the transformative power of faith and the beauty of a heart open to the boundless truth.
