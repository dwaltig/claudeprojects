# Do Not Share  
# Do Not Share  
Inspired by the profound verses of Chapter 10 from the Lotus Sutra, "Do Not Share" is a powerful Blues/Folk song that delves into the depths of wisdom, faith, and spiritual awakening. The song features raw male vocals accompanied by an evocative blend of acoustic and slide guitar, Dobro, harmonica, and bass, creating a rich and soulful sound. The steady rhythm is driven by shakers and an upbeat tempo, while the keyboard and backup vocals add depth and harmony.  
  
Through its thoughtful lyrics, the song explores the essence of higher knowledge and the path of true insight, accessible only to those with unwavering faith. With verses that caution against pride and indulgence, "Do Not Share" serves as a reminder of the transformative power of wisdom and the guiding light of the Sutra.  
  
Let the music take you on a journey through the teachings of the Lotus Sutra, where the embrace of wisdom aligns the spirit and illuminates the way from darkness to light.
