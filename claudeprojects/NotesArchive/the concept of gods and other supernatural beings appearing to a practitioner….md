the concept of gods and other supernatural beings appearing to a practitioner along the path of awakening is recognized in some forms of Buddhism, but these beings are not necessarily seen as divine gods, but rather as different realms and states of consciousness that a practitioner may encounter along the path of awakening. They may appear as tests, challenges, or guides, providing wisdom and knowledge that can aid in the attainment of enlightenment.
