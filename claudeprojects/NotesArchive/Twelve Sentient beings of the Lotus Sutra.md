Twelve Sentient beings of the Lotus Sutra  
  
1. Buddhas *reapers of correct practice*  
2. Monks *learners of correct practice*  
3. Nuns *supporters of this transcendent knowledge*  
4. Bodhisattvas *teachers/students of the Buddha's teachings*  
5. Heavenly Kings *rewarders of good diligent effort*  
6. Great Brahma Kings *fathers of creation*  
7. Dragon Kings *protectors of the sky*  
8. Kimnara Kings *the gods of music*  
9. Asura Kings *Contentious and belligerent, challenges even the gods.*  
10. Gandharva Kings *heavenly musicians living on ‘gandha,’ which means fragrance, scent, or perfume*  
11. Garuda Kings *giant birds that feeds on dragons and are regarded as the king of birds.*  
12. Human Kings, lay followers
