**Story and Message**  
* **What is the core message or story you want to convey in your song?** This will help you stay focused on the main theme.  
* **Does the song feel authentic to your own experiences or emotions?** Authenticity makes a song more relatable and impactful for listeners.  
* **What specific imagery or symbols do you use to evoke emotions and paint a picture in the listener's mind?**  
**Structure**  
* **Does the song have a strong, memorable hook?**  
* **Is there a balance between repetition and variation in the melody and lyrics?**  
* **Does the song structure effectively build and release tension?** Consider whether a traditional verse-chorus structure or a more unique structure would be most effective.  
**Emotional Engagement**  
* **Does the song create emotional peaks and valleys using dynamics?**  
* **Is there a narrative arc that culminates in an emotional climax in the chorus?**  
* **Do you use rhetorical devices, such as anaphora or antithesis, to make the lyrics resonate more deeply?**  
**Melodic and Harmonic Variety**  
* **What scales and chords are you using to create the desired mood?** Do they support the emotional tone of the song?  
* **Is the melody catchy and interesting, with surprising intervals or notes?**  
**Lyrics**  
* **Do you use metaphors and similes to make the lyrics poetic and meaningful?**
