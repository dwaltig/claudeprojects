# Offering of Merit  
  
May the merits we've gathered,   
May the merits we've gathered,   
Like seeds sown in fertile earth,   
Like seeds sown in fertile earth,   
Bloom across the vast cosmos,   
Bloom across the vast cosmos,   
Nourishing beings from their birth.  
Nourishing beings from their birth.  
  
Not for self alone we strive,   
Not for self alone we strive,   
But for life in every form.   
But for life in every form.   
Our good deeds, a shared harvest,   
Our good deeds, a shared harvest,   
A shelter from life's storm.  
A shelter from life's storm.  
  
From the humblest to the grand,   
From the humblest to the grand,   
May all climb wisdom's height.   
May all climb wisdom's height.   
Till the dawn of understanding breaks,   
Till the dawn of understanding breaks,   
Bathing all in radiant light.  
Bathing all in radiant light.  
  
In compassion's footsteps we tread,   
In compassion's footsteps we tread,   
Hand in hand, with hearts ablaze.   
Hand in hand, with hearts ablaze.   
Our offering: a world awakened,   
Our offering: a world awakened,   
Where kindness lights our days.
