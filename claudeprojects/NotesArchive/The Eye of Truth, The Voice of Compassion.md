# *The Eye of Truth, The Voice of Compassion*  
  
*The Eye of Truth, The Voice of Compassion* is a soul-stirring gospel anthem that celebrates faith, trust, and divine guidance. This uplifting song reflects on the profound wisdom of the divine, offering hope and inspiration to those seeking strength on their spiritual journey. With heartfelt lyrics and soaring melodies, it invites listeners to embrace trust in divine compassion and the unerring truth that lights our path.
