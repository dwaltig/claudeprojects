## Porter Duties  
**General Property Maintenance**  
* Inspect and maintain the overall appearance and cleanliness of the property.  
* Complete assigned daily tasks and responsibilities as outlined on job/task lists.  
* Walk the property daily to remove trash, debris, and litter from common areas.  
* Empty trash cans regularly, including those in pool areas and near mailboxes.  
* Sweep sidewalks and surrounding areas to maintain a clean and safe walking environment.  
* Remove heavy trash items and bulk debris as needed.  
    * **Take before-and-after photos of heavy trash removal for documentation.**  
* Monitor for and remove pet waste across the property.  
    * **Photo documentation is encouraged if excessive or repeated violations occur.**  
**Safety & Security**  
* Maintain a safe and secure environment for residents, staff, and visitors.  
* Check for and report cracks, damage, or uneven surfaces on sidewalks and pavement.  
* Identify and report any trip hazards or other safety concerns.  
* Apply salt or sand for ice prevention during winter months.  
* Inspect stairwells, handrails, and guardrails for stability and report issues promptly.  
* Ensure parking areas are free of litter, unauthorized vehicles, and that fire lanes are clear.  
* Regularly inspect and test emergency phone systems (monthly).  
* Verify all safety and informational signage is present and in good condition.  
**Pool & Outdoor Amenity Areas**  
* Skim pools and clean pool fixtures as needed.  
* Ensure pool gates are secured and pool water is clean.  
* Remove trash and debris from pool areas.  
* Clean and maintain outdoor furniture, especially during spring and fall.  
* Water lawns and landscaping in summer.  
* Remove fallen leaves and natural debris from outdoor areas in fall.  
    * **Before-and-after photos required for major cleanup or seasonal prep.**  
**Lighting & Electrical**  
* Regularly inspect exterior lighting fixtures.  
* Replace burnt-out light bulbs as necessary.  
    * **Photo evidence required for replaced or repaired fixtures.**  
* Inspect lighting in stairwells and other common areas.  
**Structural & Equipment Inspections**  
* Ensure areas around transformers and A/C units are free from debris.  
* Inspect and perform minor repairs to perimeter fencing and dumpster enclosures.  
    * **Photos before and after repairs should be submitted.**  
* Check for signs of vandalism or graffiti and report for removal.  
    * **Include photo documentation when reporting.**  
* Inspect for signs of pest activity and report any infestations.  
**Seasonal & Special Tasks**  
* Pressure wash sidewalks and walkways as scheduled or requested.  
* Power wash and refresh outdoor furniture in the spring.  
* Clear gutters and downspouts of leaves and debris.  
* Assist with snow/ice removal as applicable to climate.  
    * **Before-and-after images required for seasonal or weather-related work.**
