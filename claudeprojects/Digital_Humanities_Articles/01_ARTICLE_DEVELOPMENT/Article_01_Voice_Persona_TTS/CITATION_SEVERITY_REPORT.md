## Major Formatting Issues (Severity Level 2)

### 4. Page Ranges: Hyphens Instead of En-Dashes
**Status**: ✅ RESOLVED

**Problem**: Chicago 17th requires en-dashes (–) in page ranges, not hyphens (-). Found 23 instances across journal articles and book chapters.

**Examples of Corrections**:
- Bingenheimer (2016): "539-76" → "539–76"
- Bingenheimer & Hung (2018): "23-42" → "23–42"
- Benjamin (1996): "253-63" → "253–63"
- Campbell & Black (1997): "279-92" → "279–92"
- Feng et al. (2020): "391-414" → "391–414"
- Hockey (2004): "3-19" → "3–19"
- [20+ additional entries corrected]

**Resolution**: Systematically replaced all hyphens with en-dashes in page ranges across entire bibliography.
