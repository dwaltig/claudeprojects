## üî¨ Translation Verification Queries

### Comparative Translation Analysis
```
Research how major English translators have rendered [SPECIFIC TERM/PHRASE] 
from [SOURCE LANGUAGE: Classical Chinese/Pali/Sanskrit] in [SUTRA NAME].

Include analysis from:
- Academic translators (Thurman, Watson, Muller, Red Pine, Thanissaro)
- Historical translations (19th-early 20th century)
- Contemporary scholarly debates

Focus on:
1. The range of English renderings used
2. Arguments for/against each translation choice
3. Philosophical implications of different translations
4. Any consensus in contemporary scholarship
```

### Etymology Deep Dive
```
Conduct a comprehensive etymological analysis of the term [TERM] 
([ORIGINAL SCRIPT]) as used in [TRADITION: Mahayana/Theravada] Buddhism.

Research:
1. Sanskrit/Pali origins and cognates
2. How the term evolved in translation to Chinese/Tibetan
3. Semantic range and contextual variations
4. Key scholarly sources discussing this term
5. Common translation pitfalls to avoid
```

### Passage Interpretation
```
Research academic interpretations of [SUTRA NAME], [CHAPTER/VERSE NUMBER]:
"[QUOTE OR PARAPHRASE OF PASSAGE]"

Find:
1. How major commentators (Indian, Chinese, Tibetan) interpreted this passage
2. Modern scholarly analysis and debates
3. Doctrinal significance within [TRADITION]
4. Cross-references to related passages in other sutras
5. Any textual variants or translation controversies
```
