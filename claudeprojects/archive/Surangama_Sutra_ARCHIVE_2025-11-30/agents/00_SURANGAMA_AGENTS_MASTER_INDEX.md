## Overview

The Surangama Sutra is vast and multifaceted—1,486 lines covering drama, philosophy, logic, perception science, meditation methods, and cosmic vision. No single voice or vernacular can handle all of it effectively.

Following the Buddha's example of teaching in multiple modes for different audiences and subjects, we've created **seven specialized agents**, each expert in one domain, each using the most appropriate American vernacular for their material.

**The Principle:** The Buddha was proficient in all sciences and all modes of teaching. We honor that by creating agents proficient in different domains, working together to render the complete Surangama accessible.

## How the Agents Work Together

### Principle of Coordination

**Each agent handles what they do best.** No single vernacular tries to force-fit inappropriate material. **Venerable Tenzin Jing** acts as the final check.

**Example - A Complex Section:**

**Section:** Buddha investigates where consciousness is located, then explains the six sense faculties, then gives meditation instructions using the ear-door

**Agent Coordination:**
1. **Sarah Martinez** (Legal): Systematically refutes Ananda's seven wrong answers about where mind is
2. **Dr. James Chen** (Philosophical): Explains the philosophical framework of consciousness and location
3. **Dr. Maya Patel** (Scientific): Breaks down how hearing actually works neurologically and phenomenologically
4. **Master John Kim** (Practice): Gives step-by-step instructions for Avalokiteśvara's sound-door meditation
5. **Kai Reed** (Blues): Provides the emotional/metaphorical synthesis in verse
6. **Venerable Tenzin Jing** (Lineage): Reviews the whole sequence to ensure the "Taste of Dharma" is preserved.
