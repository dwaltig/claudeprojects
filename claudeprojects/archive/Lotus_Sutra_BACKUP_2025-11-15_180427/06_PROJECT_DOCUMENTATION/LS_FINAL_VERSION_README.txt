WHAT IS THIS FILE?
================================================================================

LS_final_version.txt is a comprehensive single-document compilation containing
ALL THREE versions of the Lotus Sutra:

1. CLASSICAL CHINESE (Original Kumārajīva 406 CE)
   - The source material from classical Chinese Buddhism
   - Complete 28-chapter sutra with all traditional chapter names
   - 妙法蓮華經 (Miào Fǎ Liánhuá Jīng)

2. BLUES INTERPRETATION (Contemporary Vernacular)
   - Modern blues/gospel-inflected English version
   - By William Altig
   - Same 28-chapter structure as original
   - Designed to be spoken aloud, culturally resonant
   - Gender-inclusive language while maintaining narrative power

3. SCHOLARLY ENGLISH TRANSLATION (Modern Academic)
   - Formal English translation from Kumārajīva's Classical Chinese
   - Professional scholarly apparatus
   - Includes historical preface by Dao Xuan (Tang Dynasty)
   - Proper Sanskrit diacriticals throughout
   - Academic tone suitable for scholars and serious students
