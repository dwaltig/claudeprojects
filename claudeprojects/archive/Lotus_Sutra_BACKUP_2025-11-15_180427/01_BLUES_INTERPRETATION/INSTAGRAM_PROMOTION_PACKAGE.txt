HASHTAG RESEARCH TIPS
================================================================================

Check hashtag performance:
1. Search a hashtag on Instagram
2. Look at "Top Posts" and "Recent Posts"
3. See how many posts use it
4. If 100K-1M posts: Use it (good reach)
5. If 10K-100K posts: Excellent niche match
6. If under 10K: Very niche (followers will see you)

Your hashtags should mix:
- 5-10 large hashtags (500K+ posts)
- 10-15 medium hashtags (100K-500K posts)
- 5-10 small hashtags (10K-100K posts)

This balances reach and relevance!
