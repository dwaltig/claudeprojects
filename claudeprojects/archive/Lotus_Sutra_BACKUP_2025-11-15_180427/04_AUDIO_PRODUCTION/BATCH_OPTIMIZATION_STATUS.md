## OPTIMIZATION METHODOLOGY

### 4-Rule Verse Formatting System

**Rule 1: Identify Poetry**
- Find all blocks with multiple short, intentional line breaks (poems, verses, rhythmic sections)

**Rule 2: Combine All Lines Into ONE Paragraph**
- Never leave verse lines separated
- Combine into single continuous line

**Rule 3: Preserve Pacing With Punctuation**
- Lines ending WITH punctuation (. ? ; ! : —): Keep punctuation + add space
- Lines ending WITHOUT punctuation: Replace line break with comma + space (, )

**Rule 4: Keep Everything Else Unchanged**
- Standard paragraphs/prose remain exactly as-is
- No word changes
- No meaning changes
- 100% preservation of content
