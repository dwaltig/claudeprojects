## TECHNICAL NOTES

### Optimization Method:

The optimization process:
1. Identified verse sections by detecting patterns of short lines with intentional breaks
2. Combined all verse lines within each section into single paragraphs
3. Preserved all original punctuation marks (periods, commas, dashes, etc.)
4. Added commas ONLY where original line breaks had no punctuation
5. Left all narrative prose completely unchanged

### Why This Matters for TTS:

- **Consistent Voice Tone:** Single-paragraph verses prevent unintended pauses/inflection changes
- **Natural Pacing:** Commas preserve intended breath points from line breaks
- **Processing Efficiency:** Fewer line breaks = more predictable TTS output
- **Meaning Preservation:** All words and punctuation maintained = identical meaning

### Character Count Notes:

- Spaces added between combined lines
- This is expected and correct behavior
