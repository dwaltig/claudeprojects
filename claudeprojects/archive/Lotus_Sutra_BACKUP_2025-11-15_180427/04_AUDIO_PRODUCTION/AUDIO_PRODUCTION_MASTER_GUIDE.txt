OPTION A: GEMINI API VOICE SYNTHESIS (Fastest)
Timeline: 1-2 weeks total
Cost: $300-1,000
Process:
  1. Prepare API credentials (1-2 days)
  2. Script voice tag processing (2-3 days)
  3. Process all 28 chapters through Gemini API (4-6 hours)
  4. Post-process and normalize audio (2-3 days)
  5. Add chapter markers and metadata (1-2 days)
  6. Quality assurance pass (1-2 days)

Advantages:
  - Fastest time to market
  - Lowest cost
  - Consistent voice quality
  - Easy to revise (just re-run)
  - Professional narration ready

Disadvantages:
  - Less human interpretation
  - May need manual editing for emotion/pacing
  - Requires technical setup

OPTION B: HUMAN VOICE ACTORS (Higher Quality)
Timeline: 4-8 weeks total
Cost: $6,000-23,000
Process:
  1. Casting session (1 week)
  2. Schedule recording sessions by voice (2-3 weeks)
  3. Record in professional studio (40-60 hours)
  4. Edit and assemble (1 week)
  5. Mixing and balancing (1 week)
  6. Mastering (3-5 days)
  7. Quality assurance (1-2 days)

Advantages:
  - Maximum human expression and emotion
  - Authentic pronunciation of Sanskrit/pinyin
  - Can adjust interpretation during recording
  - Professional actor experience
  - High production value

Disadvantages:
  - Much longer timeline
  - Significantly higher cost
  - Scheduling complexity (15 voice actors)
  - Requires experienced audio engineer/director

RECOMMENDED APPROACH:
HYBRID - Use Gemini API for initial draft, then consider selective human re-recording for key passages (Buddha's teaching moments, important parables) if budget allows.
