TONE SYSTEM OVERVIEW
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Mandarin Chinese uses four tones plus neutral tone:

TONE 1 (一聲) - HIGH LEVEL:        Flat, high pitch         ā ē ī ō ū
TONE 2 (二聲) - RISING:            Like asking "what?"      á é í ó ú
TONE 3 (三聲) - FALLING-RISING:    Dip then rise            ǎ ě ǐ ǒ ǔ
TONE 4 (四聲) - FALLING:           Sharp drop, emphatic     à è ì ò ù
NEUTRAL (輕聲) - UNSTRESSED:       Short, light             a e i o u

VISUAL REPRESENTATION:

Tone 1:  ———  (high and flat)
Tone 2:  ╱    (rising)
Tone 3:  ∨    (dip and rise)
Tone 4:  ╲    (falling)

PRIMARY TITLE: 妙法蓮華經
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

PINYIN: Miào Fǎ Liánhuá Jīng

PRONUNCIATION:
   Miào    = mee-OW (like cat sound, 4th tone)
   Fǎ      = fah (3rd tone - dip and rise)
   Liánhuá = lee-AHN hwah (both 2nd tone)
   Jīng    = jing (1st tone - high level)

SYLLABLE BREAKDOWN:
   妙  Miào   [miɑʊ̯˥˩]    wonderful, subtle, mysterious
   法  Fǎ     [fä˨˩˦]      dharma, law, method
   蓮  Lián   [ljɛn˧˥]     lotus
   華  huá    [xwä˧˥]      flower, splendid
   經  Jīng   [tɕiŋ˥˥]     sutra, scripture, classic

MEANING: "The Sutra of the Wonderful Dharma Lotus Flower"

ENGLISH APPROXIMATION: "mee-OW fah lee-AHN hwah jing"

CONTEXT: This is Kumārajīva's Classical Chinese title (406 CE), standard in
East Asian Buddhism. Short form: 法華經 (Fǎhuá Jīng / "Lotus Sutra").

PINYIN SPELLING CONVENTIONS

zh ch sh r     [ʈʂ ʈʂʰ ʂ ʐ] (retroflex)

FINAL VOWELS:
a  [ä]    like "ah"
o  [wɔ]   like "aw" in "law"
e  [ɤ]    like "uh" in "duh"
i  [i]    like "ee" in "see"
u  [u]    like "oo" in "boot"
ü  [y]    like German "ü" or French "u"

DIPHTHONGS:
ai [aɪ̯]   like "eye"
ei [eɪ̯]   like "ay" in "day"
ao [ɑʊ̯]   like "ow" in "cow"
ou [oʊ̯]   like "oh"

SPECIAL COMBINATIONS:
-ng [ŋ]    like "ng" in "sing"
-n  [n]    like "n" in "sun"

================================================================================

Sanskrit names use diacritical marks to indicate precise pronunciation.
All Sanskrit diacritics have been PRESERVED in the production manuscript.

DIACRITICAL MARKS GUIDE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

VOWELS:
ā = long "a" (like "father")      [aː]
ī = long "i" (like "machine")     [iː]
ū = long "u" (like "rude")        [uː]
ṛ = vocalic "r" (like "rib")      [r̩]
ṝ = long vocalic "r"              [r̩ː]
ḷ = vocalic "l" (rare)            [l̩]

CONSONANTS:
ś = palatal "sh" (like "shun")    [ɕ]
ṣ = retroflex "sh" (curl tongue)  [ʂ]
ñ = palatal "n" (like "canyon")   [ɲ]
ṅ = velar "n" (like "sing")       [ŋ]
ṇ = retroflex "n"                 [ɳ]
ṭ = retroflex "t"                 [ʈ]
ḍ = retroflex "d"                 [ɖ]
ṁ = anusvāra (nasal)              [m̃]
ḥ = visarga (aspiration)          [h]

PATHS AND VEHICLES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

dharma (法)
   PRONUNCIATION: DHAR-mah
   IPA: [dʱɐrmɐ]
   MEANING (lowercase): phenomenon, element, thing, mental object
   CONTEXT: Technical term for constituents of experience

Dharma (法)
   PRONUNCIATION: DHAR-mah
   IPA: [dʱɐrmɐ]
   MEANING (uppercase): The Teaching, Buddhist doctrine, cosmic law
   CONTEXT: The Buddha's teachings collectively

nirvāṇa (涅槃)
   PRONUNCIATION: neer-VAH-nah
   IPA: [nɪrvaːɳɐ]
   MEANING: Liberation, extinction (of suffering/craving)
   CONTEXT: Ultimate goal of Buddhist practice
   CHINESE: nièpán (涅槃)

saṃsāra (生死輪迴)
   PRONUNCIATION: sahm-SAH-rah
   IPA: [sɐmsaːrɐ]
   MEANING: Cycle of rebirth, wandering existence
   CONTEXT: The world of suffering beings traverse
   CHINESE: Shēngsǐ lúnhuí (生死輪迴)

bodhi (菩提)
   PRONUNCIATION: BOH-dee
   IPA: [boːdʱɪ]
   MEANING: Awakening, enlightenment
   CONTEXT: The realization buddhas and bodhisattvas attain
   CHINESE: pútí (菩提)

anuttara-samyak-saṃbodhi (阿耨多羅三藐三菩提)
   PRONUNCIATION: ah-NOOT-tah-rah SAHM-yahk sahm-BOH-dee
   IPA: [ɐnʊttɐrɐ sɐmjɐk sɐmboːdʱɪ]
   MEANING: Supreme perfect enlightenment
   CONTEXT: Full buddhahood (not just arhat liberation)
   CHINESE: ānòuduōluó sānmiǎo sānpútí (阿耨多羅三藐三菩提)

   CHINESE: zhēnyán (真言) or zhòu (咒)

GEMINI AI VOICE SYNTHESIS NOTES
================================================================================

FOR CHINESE PINYIN:
- Gemini will read pinyin phonetically using English rules
- Tone marks will be interpreted as prosodic/pitch cues
- Result: Phonetic approximation of Chinese (acceptable quality)
- Example: "Miào Fǎ Liánhuá Jīng" → "mee-OW fah lee-AHN-hwah jing"

FOR SANSKRIT DIACRITICS:
- Gemini will approximate Sanskrit sounds using English phonemes
- ś → "SH", ṇ → "N", ū → "OO", ā → "AH", ṃ → "M"
- Result: Anglicized but consistent Sanskrit pronunciation
- Example: "Śāriputra" → "shah-REE-poo-trah"

BOTH approaches produce intelligible, consistent audio suitable for
professional audiobook production with English-speaking audiences.
