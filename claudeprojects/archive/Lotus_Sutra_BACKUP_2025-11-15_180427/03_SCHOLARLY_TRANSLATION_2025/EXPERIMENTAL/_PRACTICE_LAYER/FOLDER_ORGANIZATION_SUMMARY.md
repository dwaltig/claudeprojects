## How to Use This Organization

### **For Individual Practitioners**
1. Start: `00_START_HERE/INDEX_READ_ME_FIRST.md`
2. Choose path based on time available
3. Follow structure in chosen folder
4. Use `03_Daily_Practice/` for ongoing engagement

### **For Study Group Facilitators**
1. Read: `00_START_HERE/PRACTITIONER_GUIDE_INTRODUCTION.md`
2. Go to: `05_Group_Practice/TEACHING_CIRCLES_FACILITATION_GUIDE.md`
3. Reference: `02_Chapter_Study_Guides/` for discussion questions

### **For Meditation Practitioners**
1. Start: `00_START_HERE/INDEX_READ_ME_FIRST.md`
2. Explore: `04_Meditation_Practice/MEDITATION_FOCAL_POINTS_COMPILED.md`
3. Choose progression (Beginner ‚Üí Advanced)
4. Practice 1-2 focal points for weeks

### **For Complete, Deep Engagement**
1. Use: `01_Study_Programs/FOUR_WEEK_INTENSIVE_STUDY_PROGRAM.md` (first 4 weeks)
2. Then: `02_Chapter_Study_Guides/` (deepen key chapters, 4 months)
3. Add: `03_Daily_Practice/` (ongoing)
4. Integrate: `04_Meditation_Practice/` (specific practices)
