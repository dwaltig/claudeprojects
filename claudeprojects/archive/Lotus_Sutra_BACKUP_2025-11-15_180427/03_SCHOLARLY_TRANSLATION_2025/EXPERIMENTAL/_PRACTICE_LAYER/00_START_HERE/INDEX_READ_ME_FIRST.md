## Tips for Success

### **Start Small**
Don't try to do everything. Choose ONE approach:
- Daily extracts (5 min), OR
- Chapter study with journaling (45 min), OR
- Group study (90 min weekly), OR
- Intensive program (60 min daily)
- Pick one and commit for at least 4 weeks before expanding

### **Build Consistency**
- Same time daily is more powerful than longer practice
- 5 minutes daily > 1 hour weekly
- The dharma rewards sustained engagement

### **Track Your Journey**
- Keep the reflection journal
- You'll see transformation you might otherwise miss
- Over months, the shifts become profound

### **Connect with Community**
- Even solo practitioners benefit from occasional connection
- Use teaching circles guide to find or create a group
- Dharma practice naturally wants to be shared

### **Return to Core Documents**
- Come back to PRACTITIONER_GUIDE_INTRODUCTION.md regularly
- It contains principles that deepen over time
- What seemed abstract first read becomes lived truth
