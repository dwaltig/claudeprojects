    1. Morning (10 min): Daily Dharma Extract + sit silently

Tips for Success

Start Small

Don't try to do everything. Choose ONE approach:
    1. Daily extracts (5 min), OR
    2. Chapter study with journaling (45 min), OR
    3. Group study (90 min weekly), OR
    4. Intensive program (60 min daily)
    5. Pick one and commit for at least 4 weeks before expanding

Build Consistency

    1. Same time daily is more powerful than longer practice
    2. 5 minutes daily > 1 hour weekly
    3. The dharma rewards sustained engagement

Track Your Journey

    1. Keep the reflection journal
    2. You'll see transformation you might otherwise miss
    3. Over months, the shifts become profound

Connect with Community

    1. Even solo practitioners benefit from occasional connection
    2. Use teaching circles guide to find or create a group
    3. Dharma practice naturally wants to be shared

Return to Core Documents

    1. Come back to PRACTITIONERGUIDEINTRODUCTION.md regularly
    2. It contains principles that deepen over time
    3. What seemed abstract first read becomes lived truth
