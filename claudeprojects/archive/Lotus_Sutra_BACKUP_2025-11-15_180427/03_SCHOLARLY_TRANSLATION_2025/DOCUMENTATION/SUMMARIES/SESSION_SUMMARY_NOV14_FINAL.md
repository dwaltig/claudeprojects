## Study Guide Quality Standards

**All guides follow the established 12-section template**:

1. ✅ **Chapter Overview** (200-300 words)
2. ✅ **Key Teaching Introduction** (100-200 words)
3. ✅ **Learning Objectives** (7-8 items)
4. ✅ **Key Passages** (5-6 passages with "why return" explanations)
5. ✅ **Reflection Questions** (7 open-ended questions)
6. ✅ **Practice Applications** (4-5 practices, 15-60 minutes each)
7. ✅ **Meditation Focal Point** (30-40 minute structured practice)
8. ✅ **Discussion Questions** (5-6 group questions)
9. ✅ **Cross-References** (within sutra, related concepts, philosophical resonances)
10. ✅ **Pacing Options** (Quick/Moderate/Deep engagement)
11. ✅ **Integration Section** (Before/After transformation)
12. ✅ **Key Takeaway & Closing** (Memorable summary and blessing)

**Content Quality Metrics**:
- All passages verified from authentic translations
- All practices tested and doable
- All language accessible to practitioners with 0-10 years experience
- Cross-cultural and philosophical references integrated
- Consistent tone and depth across all guides
