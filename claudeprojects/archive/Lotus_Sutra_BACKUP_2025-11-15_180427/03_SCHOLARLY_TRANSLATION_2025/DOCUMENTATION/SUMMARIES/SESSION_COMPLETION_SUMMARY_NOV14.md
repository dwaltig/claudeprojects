## Part 2: Advanced Practice Methodology ✅

### **BODHISATTVA_PATH_METHODOLOGY_EXTRACTED.md** (15,000+ words)

**What It Is:**
A comprehensive guide extracting six bodhisattva practice methodologies from Lotus Sutra Chapters 23-28, showing how to progressively embody enlightened approaches to practice.

**The Six Stages:**
1. **Medicine King** — The Way of Complete Offering
   - Stages: External offerings → Service → Intellectual offering → Vow offering → Self-offering
   - Duration: 6+ months for foundation, lifetime for deepening

2. **Wonderful-Sound** — The Way of Manifesting Forms
   - Stages: Perception of multiplicity → Flexibility → Embodied manifestation → Teaching presence
   - Duration: 6+ months to develop flexibility

3. **Avalokiteśvara** — The Way of Universal Compassion
   - Stages: Receiving compassion → Healing → Listening → Responding → Specialized forms
   - Duration: 3-6 months per significant wound; lifetime deepening

4. **Dharani Guardians** — The Way of Protective Presence
   - Stages: Personal sanctuary → Protecting others → Protecting dharma → Dispelling harm
   - Duration: 40 days to establish; ongoing refinement

5. **Wonderful-Adornment King** — The Way of Skillful Teaching
   - Stages: Perception → Method matching → Working with resistance → Teaching through being
   - Duration: 6 months to years for mastery

6. **Samantabhadra** — The Way of Eternal Vows
   - Stages: Four conditions → Personal vows → Eternal support → Protecting future practitioners
   - Duration: Lifetime commitment; infinite progression

**Key Features:**
- Each stage has specific, doable practices
- Stages build progressively; each foundation enables the next
- 3-year suggested timeline for engaging all six
- Balances aspiration with practical implementation
- Explicitly connects to embodiment of bodhisattva qualities
- Includes self-assessment for recognizing progress
- Appropriate for both individual and group practice

**Who Benefits:**
- Practitioners with 2+ years experience seeking structured advancement
- Those interested in bodhisattva path and real-world embodiment
- Teachers wanting to guide others through systematic development
- Anyone ready to commit to multi-year transformative practice
