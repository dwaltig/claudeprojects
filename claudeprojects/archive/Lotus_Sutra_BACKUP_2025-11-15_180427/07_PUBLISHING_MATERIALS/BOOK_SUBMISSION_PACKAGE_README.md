## üí° PRO TIPS FOR SUCCESS

1. **Personalize each query letter** - Research the publisher and editor
2. **Customize for each publisher's mission** - Show you understand them
3. **Emphasize different angles** - Buddhist scholars vs. general readers vs. activists
4. **Follow submission guidelines exactly** - Don't guess at what they want
5. **Proofread everything** - Typos hurt your credibility
6. **Be patient** - 6-8 week response time is normal
7. **Follow up professionally** - One polite follow-up after 6 weeks is okay
8. **Keep records** - Track where you've submitted
9. **Be prepared to respond quickly** - If they're interested, respond promptly
10. **Believe in your work** - This is a genuinely good book that will find an audience
